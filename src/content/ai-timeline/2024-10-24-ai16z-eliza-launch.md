ai16z — named as a parody of a16z — launched on Solana as the first AI-managed decentralized venture fund. Built by Shaw Walters, the project's AI agent "Eliza" makes investment decisions autonomously, using the open-source ElizaOS framework. Within weeks it became one of the largest AI tokens by market cap and spawned an ecosystem of thousands of AI agents. The ElizaOS framework became the de facto standard for building crypto-native AI agents.
