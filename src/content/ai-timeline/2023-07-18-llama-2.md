Meta released Llama 2 on July 18, 2023, in partnership with Microsoft, making it freely available for research and commercial use. Llama 2 models (7B, 13B, 34B, 70B) were significantly better than Llama 1 and included instruction-tuned chat variants. The commercial license covered most businesses with under 700M monthly users, making it the most permissive frontier open model to date. Llama 2 became the dominant open-source base model for enterprise fine-tuning and local deployment, validating Meta's open strategy.
