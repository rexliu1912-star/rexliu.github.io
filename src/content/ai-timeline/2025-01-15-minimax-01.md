MiniMax open-sources MiniMax-01, a hybrid Mixture-of-Experts model with 1 million token context window, trained with custom reinforcement learning (CISPO). Trained at a fraction of typical frontier costs.

**So what?** MiniMax didn't just match DeepSeek on cost efficiency — they open-sourced a 1M context model when GPT-4 was limited to 128K. For any application needing full-book or full-codebase context, this was a paradigm shift. Chinese labs were no longer followers; they were setting the bar on long-context architecture.
