Baidu launches ERNIE 4.5 (native multimodal, starting at RMB 0.004 per 1K tokens) and ERNIE X1 (deep-thinking reasoning model) — while simultaneously making ERNIE Bot free for consumers.

**So what?** DeepSeek's low-cost shock forced every Chinese AI lab to reprice. Baidu's response was dual: a cheaper multimodal frontier model AND a reasoning model to match the o1/R1 wave. Going free on the consumer app is the right strategic move — ERNIE needs users to stay relevant in China's AI chatbot wars, where Doubao and Kimi are both growing fast.
