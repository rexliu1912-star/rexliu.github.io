Meta launched Meta AI, its AI assistant powered by the Meta Llama 3 model, on April 18, 2024. Meta AI was embedded directly into Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger, as well as Meta Ray-Ban smart glasses. Users could interact with Meta AI via text, voice, and the camera on smart glasses. At launch, Meta AI could search the web in real time and generate images through Instagram. The rollout made Meta AI one of the most widely distributed AI assistants globally.
