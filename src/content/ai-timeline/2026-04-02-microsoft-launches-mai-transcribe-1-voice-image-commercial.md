Microsoft AI announced MAI-Transcribe-1, a new speech-to-text model, and made MAI-Voice-1 and MAI-Image-2 broadly available for commercial use through Microsoft Foundry and MAI Playground. The three models cover transcription, voice generation, and image generation — positioning Microsoft as a full multimodal AI provider independent of OpenAI.

<!-- zh -->

微软 AI 部门（由 Mustafa Suleiman 领导）于 4 月 2 日宣布 MAI-Transcribe-1 正式发布，同时将 MAI-Voice-1 和 MAI-Image-2 开放给开发者用于商业场景。三款模型均可通过 Microsoft Foundry 和 MAI Playground 调用，定位为高质量、高性价比的多模态 AI 能力，覆盖语音转文字、语音生成和图像生成三个方向。这标志着微软在 AI 模型层的自主研发能力进一步独立于 OpenAI。
