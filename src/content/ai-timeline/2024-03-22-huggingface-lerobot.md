Hugging Face launches LeRobot, an open-source library for real-world robotics machine learning in PyTorch, led by former Tesla scientist Remi Cadene. Includes models, datasets, and simulation environments on the Hub.

**So what?** Hugging Face, which democratized NLP and then vision AI, turns its model-sharing flywheel toward physical intelligence. LeRobot is to robotics what Transformers.py was to language models: a single library that packages best-in-class algorithms for anyone to run. As physical AI heats up, whoever owns the open robotics stack controls the research default.
