Microsoft launched the new AI-powered Bing on February 7, 2023, integrating OpenAI's GPT-4 model into its search engine. The new Bing featured a conversational chat interface alongside traditional search results, allowing users to ask complex questions and get synthesized answers. Microsoft positioned Bing Chat as a "co-pilot for the web," marking the most significant challenge to Google's search dominance in decades. The launch ignited an AI search war and was later renamed Microsoft Copilot.
