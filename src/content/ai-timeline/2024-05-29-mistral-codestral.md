Mistral launches Codestral (22B), its first code-specialized model. Designed for code completion and generation across 80+ programming languages, with fill-in-the-middle (FIM) support.

**So what?** Mistral signaled it's not just playing "open-source GPT" — it's carving out developer-specific vertical markets. Codestral integrates with VS Code, JetBrains, and Continue, making it competitive with GitHub Copilot for developers who prefer local or EU-hosted models. Europe's first serious contender in the coding AI race.
