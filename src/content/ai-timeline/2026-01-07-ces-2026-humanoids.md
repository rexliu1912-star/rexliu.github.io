CES 2026 became the "Year of the Humanoid Robot," with every major robotics company showcasing next-generation systems. Highlights included Boston Dynamics' Atlas commercial debut, NEURA Robotics' 4NE1 Gen 3 (cognitive humanoid), AgiBot's consumer-facing Genie, and dozens of other entrants. The sheer volume of humanoid demonstrations signaled that the robotics industry had crossed from research curiosity to commercial product cycle.
