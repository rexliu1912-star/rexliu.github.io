ByteDance's Doubao surpasses all competitors to become China's most-used AI chatbot, reaching 155 million weekly active users according to QuestMobile data from late 2025 — while Volcano Engine, ByteDance's enterprise tech arm, targets RMB 25B+ revenue in 2025.

**So what?** ByteDance already cracked distribution once with TikTok/Douyin. Applying the same playbook to AI — deep TikTok-native integration, aggressive monetization through Volcano Engine, and relentless product iteration — works. Doubao's user lead reflects ByteDance's systemic advantage: it doesn't just have an AI product, it has China's largest content distribution network.
