Google announced Bard, its experimental conversational AI service powered by LaMDA (Language Model for Dialogue Applications), on February 6, 2023 — just days after Microsoft launched the new AI-powered Bing. Bard was initially made available to trusted testers before opening to the public in March. The announcement signaled Google's urgency to compete in the AI chatbot space, though an early demo error briefly knocked $100B off Alphabet's market cap, highlighting the risks of rushed AI deployment.
