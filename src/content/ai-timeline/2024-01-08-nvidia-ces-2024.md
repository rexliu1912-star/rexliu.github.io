At CES 2024, NVIDIA showcased its Avatar Cloud Engine (ACE) technology enabling game developers to create AI-powered NPCs with dynamic, personalized conversations using natural language. NVIDIA also demonstrated digital human technologies through its Tokkio and Audio2Face platforms, showing photorealistic AI avatars capable of real-time conversation. The announcements highlighted NVIDIA's expansion beyond training chips into real-time AI inference for interactive applications. CEO Jensen Huang's keynote drew massive crowds as NVIDIA's AI dominance was at its peak.
