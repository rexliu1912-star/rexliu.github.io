ByteDance releases Doubao 2.0, an advanced model positioned for the "agent era" — designed to execute complex, multi-step real-world tasks rather than just answering questions.

**So what?** Doubao 2.0 is ByteDance's agentic pivot: from chatbot to AI operator. With TikTok-scale distribution behind it and Volcano Engine's enterprise contracts, ByteDance is moving up the value chain from model provider to task executor. If Doubao 2.0 can run workflows inside enterprise systems the way Doubao runs feeds on phones, ByteDance becomes a genuine enterprise AI threat.
