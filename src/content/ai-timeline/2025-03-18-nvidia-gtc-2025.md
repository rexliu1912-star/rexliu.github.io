At GTC 2025, Jensen Huang unveiled NVIDIA's next two GPU generations: Blackwell Ultra, an enhanced version of the Blackwell architecture with improved memory and compute for AI training workloads; and Vera Rubin, the follow-on architecture combining the Vera CPU with the Rubin GPU for a new era of AI supercomputing. NVIDIA also expanded its NIM microservices ecosystem and announced new Cosmos robotics simulation capabilities. The keynote solidified NVIDIA's roadmap dominance in the AI accelerator market for the coming years.
