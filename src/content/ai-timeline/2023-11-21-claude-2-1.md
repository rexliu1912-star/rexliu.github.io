Anthropic released Claude 2.1, doubling the context window to 200,000 tokens — the longest of any publicly available AI model at the time. The model also featured reduced hallucination rates and improved instruction following. Claude 2.1 could process entire codebases, financial reports, or legal documents in a single context. The 200K window set a new standard for enterprise long-document AI applications.
