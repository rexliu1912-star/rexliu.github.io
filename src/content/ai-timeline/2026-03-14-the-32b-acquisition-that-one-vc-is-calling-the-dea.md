Index Ventures Partner Shardul Shah says cybersecurity startup Wiz sits "at the center of three tailwinds: AI, cloud, and security spend." Those tailwinds powered what just became the largest venture-backed acquisition in history — Google's $32 billion deal for Wiz, finalized after Wiz declined Google's earlier $23B offer in 2024.

**So what?** Cybersecurity is now table stakes for enterprise AI. By acquiring Wiz, Google gets the leading cloud security platform and closes a major gap against Microsoft's security portfolio. This is a bet that the next wave of AI adoption runs through securing cloud infrastructure.
