OpenAI closed a $6.6 billion funding round led by Thrive Capital, valuing the company at $157 billion — making it one of the most valuable private companies in the world. The round included investments from Microsoft, NVIDIA, SoftBank, and others. The capital was earmarked for compute infrastructure, research, and continued product development. The raise came as OpenAI was exploring a transition from its nonprofit-controlled structure to a for-profit benefit corporation, raising governance questions across the AI industry.
