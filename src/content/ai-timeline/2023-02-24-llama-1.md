Meta released LLaMA (Large Language Model Meta AI) on February 24, 2023, a collection of foundation language models ranging from 7B to 65B parameters. Unlike proprietary models, LLaMA was released for research purposes to democratize access to large language models. Despite a research-only license, LLaMA weights leaked online within days, sparking an explosion of open-source AI development. LLaMA kickstarted the open-source LLM movement, enabling Alpaca, Vicuna, and dozens of community fine-tunes that accelerated AI research globally.
