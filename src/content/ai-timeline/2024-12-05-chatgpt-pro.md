OpenAI launched ChatGPT Pro, a premium $200/month subscription tier offering unlimited access to its most advanced models including o1 Pro Mode. The Pro tier was designed for power users requiring the highest performance with no rate limits. It included access to OpenAI's extended thinking capabilities via o1 Pro, which used significantly more compute to solve harder problems. The launch signaled OpenAI's move toward a multi-tier commercial strategy targeting professionals and enterprise users.
