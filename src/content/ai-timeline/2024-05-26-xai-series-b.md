xAI closed a $6 billion Series B funding round on May 26, 2024, reaching a post-money valuation of $24 billion with participation from Andreessen Horowitz, Sequoia Capital, Valor Equity Partners, Vy Capital, Fidelity, and Prince Alwaleed Bin Talal. The capital was earmarked for scaling xAI's infrastructure, including the massive Colossus supercomputer cluster in Memphis, Tennessee used to train Grok models. The round established xAI as one of the best-funded AI startups globally within just one year of its founding. A second $6B round (Series C) followed in December 2024, bringing total raised to over $12 billion.
