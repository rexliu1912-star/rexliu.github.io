OpenAI releases GPT-5.4, described as "our most capable and efficient frontier model for professional work." Available in standard and Thinking (reasoning) variants, plus a Pro tier. GPT-5.4 Thinking replaces GPT-5.2 Thinking, which sunsets in 3 months.

**So what?** Two days after GPT-5.3, OpenAI ships 5.4 — the iteration speed is insane. The Thinking variant signals reasoning models are now the default frontier, not a side experiment. This is OpenAI trying to close the gap with Anthropic and Google on agentic capabilities.
