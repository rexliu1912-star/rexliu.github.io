OpenAI releases GPT-5.4 in three variants: GPT-5.3 Instant (fast everyday tasks), GPT-5.4 Thinking (professional workflows with reasoning), and GPT-5.4 Pro. Features an opt-in 1M token context window. GPT-5.4 Thinking replaces GPT-5.2 Thinking, which sunsets in 3 months.

**So what?** Two days after GPT-5.3, OpenAI ships 5.4 — the iteration speed is insane. The Thinking variant signals reasoning models are now the default frontier, not a side experiment. 1M context opt-in is a quiet but significant infrastructure shift for agent builders.
