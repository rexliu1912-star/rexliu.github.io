Stability AI released Stable Diffusion 3 Medium on June 12, 2024, making the open-weights version of its next-generation text-to-image model publicly available after months of enterprise preview. SD3 Medium uses a novel Multimodal Diffusion Transformer (MMDiT) architecture that dramatically improves text rendering within images and multi-subject scene composition. The open release democratized access to state-of-the-art image generation, with larger variants (SD3 Large and SD3 Ultra) available via the API. SD3 Medium was followed by Stable Diffusion 3.5 in October 2024, cementing Stability AI's position as the leading open-source image generation platform.
