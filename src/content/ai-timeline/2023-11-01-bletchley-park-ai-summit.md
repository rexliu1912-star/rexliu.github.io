The UK hosted the world's first global AI Safety Summit at the historic Bletchley Park — birthplace of modern computing. Representatives from 28 countries including the US, China, and EU signed the "Bletchley Declaration," acknowledging AI's existential risks. Major AI companies committed to sharing safety information with governments. A landmark moment for international AI governance.
