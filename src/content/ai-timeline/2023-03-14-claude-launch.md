Anthropic publicly launched Claude, its first AI assistant, on March 14, 2023. Claude was designed with safety as a core principle, trained using Constitutional AI (CAI) to be helpful, harmless, and honest. The launch marked Anthropic's entrance into the consumer AI market, positioning itself as a safety-focused alternative to ChatGPT. Early access was available via claude.ai and API.
