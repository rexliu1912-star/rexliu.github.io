Deepgram launches Aura-2, its enterprise text-to-speech model designed for real-time voice AI applications, with human-like expressiveness and ultra-low latency for production voice agents.

**So what?** With Nova-2 for STT and Aura-2 for TTS, Deepgram now offers a complete voice AI stack under one API. End-to-end speech-to-speech inference without stitching together multiple vendors means fewer points of failure, lower latency, and better cost economics. Voice AI agents went from prototype to production-ready.
