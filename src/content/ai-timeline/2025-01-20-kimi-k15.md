Moonshot AI releases Kimi K1.5, a reasoning-focused model that matches OpenAI o1 on mathematics, coding, and multimodal reasoning benchmarks — delivered just days before DeepSeek-R1 made global headlines.

**So what?** The week of January 20, 2025 became a proof point that China's AI labs weren't trailing by months — they were parallel. Kimi K1.5 and DeepSeek-R1 arriving simultaneously shattered the narrative that advanced reasoning models required trillion-dollar infrastructure. The reasoning capabilities race went fully global.
