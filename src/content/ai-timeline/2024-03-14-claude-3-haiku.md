Anthropic released Claude 3 Haiku, the fastest and most compact model in the Claude 3 family, designed for near-instant responsiveness in enterprise applications. Despite being the smallest Claude 3 model, Haiku outperformed many competitor models on key benchmarks. At just $0.25 per million input tokens, it offered the best price-performance ratio in the Claude lineup. Haiku was positioned as the go-to model for high-throughput, latency-sensitive production deployments.
