Google released Gemma on February 21, 2024 — a family of lightweight, open-weights AI models built from the same research and technology used to create Gemini. Available in 2B and 7B parameter sizes, Gemma models could run on laptops, workstations, or cloud infrastructure. Google provided pre-trained and instruction-tuned variants along with responsible use guidelines. Gemma became an immediate hit in the open-source AI community, offering Google-quality models that developers could freely use, fine-tune, and deploy.
