OpenAI launched Operator on January 23, 2025, a research preview of an AI agent that can browse the web and complete tasks autonomously using its own browser. Powered by the Computer-Using Agent (CUA) model, Operator can fill forms, place orders, and navigate websites without human hand-holding. It was initially available only to ChatGPT Pro users in the US, with a broader rollout planned later. Operator was later fully integrated into ChatGPT in July 2025 as "ChatGPT agent mode," signaling OpenAI's strategic shift toward agentic AI.
