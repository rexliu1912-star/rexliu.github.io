Baidu unveils ERNIE 5.0 at Baidu World 2025 — a natively omnimodal foundation model that jointly models text, images, audio, and video. Baidu announces global expansion push.

**So what?** Baidu's ERNIE 5.0 natively trains on all modalities simultaneously rather than bolting them together post-hoc. In China's enterprise AI market, Baidu retains significant distribution through its search and cloud businesses. ERNIE 5.0 signals Baidu is still competing at the frontier, even as DeepSeek and ByteDance eat its consumer share.
