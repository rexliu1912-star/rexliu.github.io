OpenAI released GPT-5.4 mini and GPT-5.4 nano, bringing many capabilities of the GPT-5.4 flagship to faster, more cost-efficient models. Both are available in the API, Codex, and ChatGPT — mini is accessible to free users. The models are designed for high-volume, latency-sensitive workloads including coding assistants and multi-modal pipelines, running more than twice as fast as previous GPT-5 mini variants.

OpenAI 发布 GPT-5.4 mini 和 nano，将旗舰模型的能力下放到更快、更高性价比的版本。两款模型同步上线 API、Codex 和 ChatGPT，免费用户也可使用 mini 版本。主打高并发、低延迟场景（代理、编码助手、多模态工作流），速度较上代 GPT-5 mini 提升 2 倍以上。
