At its first developer conference DevDay, OpenAI announced GPT-4 Turbo with a 128K context window and knowledge cutoff of April 2023, along with dramatically reduced pricing. The event also introduced custom GPTs — user-created versions of ChatGPT configured with specific instructions, knowledge, and tools. The GPT Store was announced as a forthcoming marketplace for sharing custom GPTs. DevDay marked OpenAI's transition from research lab to full-scale developer platform.
