OpenAI launched ChatGPT agent mode on July 17, 2025, integrating Operator's web browsing and computer-use capabilities directly into ChatGPT for Pro, Plus, and Team subscribers. ChatGPT can now proactively choose from a toolbox of agentic skills — browsing the web, running code, managing files — to complete complex multi-step tasks autonomously. The release effectively retired Operator as a standalone product, folding its capabilities into the main ChatGPT interface. This marked OpenAI's decisive move to transform ChatGPT from a chatbot into a fully capable autonomous AI agent.
