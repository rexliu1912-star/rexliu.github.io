Google releases Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite, available in preview via Gemini API in Google AI Studio and Vertex AI. Positioned as their most cost-effective AI model to date.

**So what?** The race to the bottom on inference cost continues. Google is making the "good enough" tier cheaper and more accessible, which matters for agent architectures where you're chaining many calls.
