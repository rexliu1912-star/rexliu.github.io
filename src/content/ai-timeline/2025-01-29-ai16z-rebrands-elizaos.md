ai16z officially rebranded to ElizaOS after the real a16z (Andreessen Horowitz) requested the name change due to brand confusion. The project simultaneously announced plans to build its own L1 blockchain optimized for AI agents. The rebranding crystallized the project's identity around its open-source agent framework, which had by this point powered thousands of AI agent deployments across crypto.
