Google released Gemma 2 on June 27, 2024, with significantly improved performance over the original Gemma models. Available in 9B and 27B parameter sizes, Gemma 2 demonstrated competitive performance with much larger models while remaining efficient enough to run on a single GPU or TPU. The models featured improvements in reasoning, code generation, and multilingual capabilities. Gemma 2 became the preferred open-weights baseline for researchers evaluating efficient AI architectures.
