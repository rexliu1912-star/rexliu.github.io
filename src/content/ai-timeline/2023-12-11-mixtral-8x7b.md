Mistral AI released Mixtral 8x7B on December 11, 2023, a sparse Mixture-of-Experts language model that uses only 2 of 8 expert networks per token, delivering GPT-3.5-level performance at a fraction of the inference cost. Released with open weights under the Apache 2.0 license, Mixtral 8x7B outperformed Llama 2 70B on most benchmarks despite using fewer active parameters. The model achieved strong multilingual performance in English, French, Italian, German, and Spanish, and excelled at code generation. Mixtral's efficient MoE architecture became one of the most influential open-source model designs of 2024, widely adopted and fine-tuned by the community.
