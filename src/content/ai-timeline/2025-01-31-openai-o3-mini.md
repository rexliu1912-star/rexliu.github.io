OpenAI released o3-mini, a cost-efficient reasoning model delivering strong performance on STEM tasks including math, science, and coding at a fraction of the compute cost of o3. The model offered three reasoning effort levels — low, medium, and high — letting developers balance speed and accuracy. o3-mini outperformed o1 on many STEM benchmarks while being significantly cheaper, making advanced reasoning accessible to a much broader set of developers and applications.
