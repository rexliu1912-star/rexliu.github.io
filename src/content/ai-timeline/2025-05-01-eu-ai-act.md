The EU AI Act's first obligations take effect, requiring transparency for AI systems and banning certain high-risk applications.

**So what?** Regulatory fragmentation begins — EU goes strict, US goes light. Companies need two playbooks. Compliance becomes a competitive advantage for some, a moat for others.
