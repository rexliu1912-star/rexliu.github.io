Mistral AI closes €600M (~$645M) Series B, bringing valuation to €5.8B ($6.2B). Backed by Andreessen Horowitz, General Catalyst, and others — cementing it as Europe's most valuable AI lab.

**So what?** In just 12 months from founding, Mistral became Europe's flagship AI champion. The raise wasn't just money — it was a geopolitical statement: Europe has a credible alternative to US hyperscalers and a potential counterweight to US/China AI duopoly narratives. The €5.8B valuation set the benchmark for sovereign AI investment.
