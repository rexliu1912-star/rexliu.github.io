Cerebras has publicly filed for a U.S. IPO, reviving its listing process after pulling back a prior attempt. The filing matters beyond one company: it brings a pure-play AI infrastructure challenger closer to public markets at a time when investors are still deciding how much value can accrue outside NVIDIA’s ecosystem.

<!-- zh -->

AI 芯片公司 Cerebras 公开提交美国 IPO 申请，重启此前暂停的上市进程。对 AI 基础设施赛道来说，这不只是一级市场融资事件，也是在公开市场测试资本是否愿意继续为非英伟达路线的大规模算力叙事买单。若上市推进顺利，Cerebras 将成为观察 AI 芯片景气度、推理需求与云算力资本开支的重要窗口。
