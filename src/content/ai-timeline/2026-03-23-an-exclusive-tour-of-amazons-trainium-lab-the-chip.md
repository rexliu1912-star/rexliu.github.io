Shortly after Amazon announced its $50 billion investment in OpenAI, AWS invited TechCrunch on a private tour of the Trainium chip lab at the heart of the deal. Trainium is Amazon's custom AI training chip, and it has won over major AI labs including Anthropic, OpenAI, and Apple as a cost-effective alternative to NVIDIA hardware.

亚马逊宣布向 OpenAI 投资 500 亿美元后，AWS 邀请 TechCrunch 对 Trainium 芯片实验室进行了独家参观。Trainium 是亚马逊自研 AI 训练芯片，已赢得 Anthropic、OpenAI、Apple 等顶级 AI 机构的采用，被视为英伟达硬件的成本效益替代方案。
