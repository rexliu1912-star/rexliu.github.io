At AWS re:Invent 2024, Amazon launches the Nova family — Micro, Lite, Pro, and Canvas/Reel — its first proprietary frontier models trained in-house and offered via Bedrock.

**So what?** Amazon had been the "neutral platform" in AI, just selling Anthropic and others. Nova signals the transition: AWS is now a model builder, not just a cloud provider. With Bedrock as the distribution channel and Trainium as the silicon stack, Amazon is betting it can compete from cloud all the way down to silicon. That's a vertically integrated threat to OpenAI's enterprise moat.
