Meta releases Llama 3.1 405B — 15 trillion tokens, 16,000 GPUs, and the first open-weight model to credibly match GPT-4 on real benchmarks. Available as API and for local deployment.

**So what?** This is the moment open-source caught up to the frontier. Any lab, researcher, or startup can now fine-tune a GPT-4 tier model without paying OpenAI. It didn't just open a door — it demolished a wall. The "open vs. closed" debate in AI permanently shifted, and every enterprise that cared about data privacy suddenly had a legitimate alternative.
