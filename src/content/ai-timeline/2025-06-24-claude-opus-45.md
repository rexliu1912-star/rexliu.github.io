Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.5, introducing hybrid reasoning — the ability to seamlessly switch between fast response mode and extended thinking mode within the same context. This allowed the model to reason deeply on complex problems without requiring separate "thinking" prompts. Claude Opus 4.5 achieved state-of-the-art scores on graduate-level reasoning benchmarks and demonstrated significant improvements in multi-step agentic tasks. The hybrid reasoning architecture became a new paradigm for frontier AI model design.
