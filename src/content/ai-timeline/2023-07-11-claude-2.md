Anthropic launched Claude 2, a major upgrade featuring improved reasoning, longer context (100K tokens), and better coding capabilities. Claude 2 scored higher than its predecessor on academic benchmarks including bar exam, GRE, and coding tests. It was made available to the public via claude.ai and API, competing directly with GPT-4. The model also showed improvements in following complex multi-step instructions.
