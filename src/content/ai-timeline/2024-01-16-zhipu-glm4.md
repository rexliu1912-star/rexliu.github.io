Zhipu AI releases GLM-4, its flagship large language model competing with GPT-4 on Chinese-language tasks and general reasoning, with multimodal and code capabilities.

**So what?** China's top AI labs didn't just close the gap on English benchmarks — GLM-4 showed strength on Chinese-specific reasoning where Western models underperform. For enterprise buyers in China, a domestically trained frontier model isn't just a preference — it's a regulatory and data sovereignty necessity. Zhipu's GLM line is China's most viable GPT-4 substitute.
