Google released Gemini 3.1 Pro on February 19, 2026, a significant upgrade to the core intelligence behind Gemini 3, featuring a 1 million token context window and state-of-the-art performance on ARC-AGI-2 (77.1%) and other advanced benchmarks. The model significantly outperforms Gemini 3 Pro across reasoning and multimodal capabilities spanning text, images, audio, video, and code. Gemini 3.1 Pro rolled out immediately to the Gemini app as well as NotebookLM for Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers. The release was part of Google's broader February 2026 AI drop that also included the Lyria 3 music generation model.
