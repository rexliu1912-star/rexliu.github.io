Canadian robotics company Sanctuary AI unveiled Phoenix, its sixth-generation general-purpose humanoid robot, alongside **Carbon** — an AI control system designed to give Phoenix human-like intelligence. Standing 5'7" and weighing 155 lbs, Phoenix could perform over 100 industrial tasks. Sanctuary's vision: robots that can learn any job a human can do, marking an early milestone in the race for commercially viable humanoid robots.
