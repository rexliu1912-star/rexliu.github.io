President Trump signed the GENIUS Act (Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for US Stablecoins Act) into law, creating the first comprehensive federal regulatory framework for US dollar-pegged stablecoins. The law established licensing requirements, reserve standards, and consumer protections — a landmark step in integrating digital assets into the mainstream financial system and directly relevant to AI payment infrastructure.
