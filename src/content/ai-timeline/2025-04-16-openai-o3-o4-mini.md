OpenAI released o3 and o4-mini on April 16, 2025, the first reasoning models with full tool access — including web browsing, Python execution, image generation, and file analysis — natively built in. o3 became OpenAI's most capable reasoning model, while o4-mini offered a strong balance of speed and performance at lower cost. Both models could "think with images" by cropping and zooming into uploaded visuals during their reasoning process. The launch represented a fundamental convergence of reasoning and tool-use capabilities in a single model.
