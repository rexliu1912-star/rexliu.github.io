Anthropic has signed a deeper infrastructure and capital agreement with Amazon: Amazon is investing another $5 billion immediately, Anthropic is committing more than $100 billion in AWS spend over the next decade, and the company will secure up to 5 gigawatts of new compute capacity. This is more than a funding round. It is a clear sign that frontier-model competition is now being locked into long-duration capital, custom silicon, and cloud-distribution partnerships.

<!-- zh -->

Anthropic 与 Amazon 签署新协议，Amazon 先追加 50 亿美元投资，未来还可再投至多 200 亿美元；同时 Anthropic 承诺未来十年在 AWS 技术上投入超过 1000 亿美元，并锁定最高 5GW 的新增算力容量。这不是普通融资，而是把资本、芯片、云平台和模型分发进一步绑成同一条利益链，也说明头部大模型公司的竞争已经从模型本身升级为算力供给与云渠道的长期绑定。
