OpenAI closed a monster $122 billion funding round, raising $3 billion from retail investors while remaining private. The round, led by Amazon, NVIDIA, and SoftBank, values the AI lab at $852 billion — approaching the world's most valuable companies — as it accelerates toward an IPO.
