OpenAI released GPT-5.2-Codex on January 14, 2026, a version of GPT-5.2 further optimized for complex, real-world software engineering tasks. It achieved state-of-the-art results on SWE-Bench Pro (56.4%) and Terminal-Bench 2.0 (64.0%), excelling at long-horizon coding tasks, large-scale code transformations, and cybersecurity challenges. The model was immediately available to paid ChatGPT users in all Codex surfaces. GPT-5.2-Codex established the new standard for AI-assisted software engineering and signaled OpenAI's intent to dominate the developer tooling market.
