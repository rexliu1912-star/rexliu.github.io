At AWS re:Invent 2025, Amazon announces Nova 2 (complete generational upgrade) and Kiro — an agentic IDE with browser automation achieving 90%+ reliability. Amazon doubles down on enterprise AI agents across Bedrock.

**So what?** AWS's agent-first posture is clear: they're not competing for creative AI or chatbot mindshare — they're competing for enterprise workflow automation budgets. Nova 2 powers Bedrock; Kiro takes aim at GitHub Copilot. Amazon's strategy is infrastructure + IDE + models: control all three layers and lock in enterprise developers.
