Ritual raised $25M in seed funding to build open, decentralized infrastructure for AI computation. The project aims to break the monopoly of large tech companies on AI access by enabling permissionless AI inference on-chain. Backed by Archetype and others, Ritual became an early pioneer of the AI×crypto infrastructure narrative.
