Moonshot AI releases Kimi K2, a 1 trillion parameter open-weight MoE model — one of the largest openly available models in existence. Achieves strong agentic task completion and tool-use benchmarks.

**So what?** Trillion-parameter open-weight models were once thought impossible outside of hyperscaler infrastructure. Kimi K2 rewrote that assumption. For the open-source community, it's the equivalent of Meta's Llama 3.1 405B moment — but orders of magnitude larger. Any researcher or startup can now run trillion-parameter intelligence locally or on cheap cloud infra.
