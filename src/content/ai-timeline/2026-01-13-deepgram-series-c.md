Deepgram closes $130M Series C at $1.3B valuation, led by AVP with participation from BlackRock, Twilio, ServiceNow, and existing investors. The company ends 2024 cash-flow positive with 400+ enterprise customers.

**So what?** Voice AI infrastructure has its first unicorn. Deepgram's cash-flow positive status is rare in AI infrastructure — proving that owning the voice layer of the AI stack is genuinely monetizable. With 200,000+ developers and growing enterprise deployments, Deepgram is becoming the "voice API layer" for the AI economy, much like Stripe is the payment layer.
