OpenAI releases o1, a model trained with reinforcement learning to "think before answering," excelling at math, coding, and science.

**So what?** The paradigm shifts from "next token prediction" to "chain-of-thought reasoning." This is the beginning of System 2 thinking in AI — a step toward AGI.
