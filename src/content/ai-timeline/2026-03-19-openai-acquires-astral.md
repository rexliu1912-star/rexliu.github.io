OpenAI announced the acquisition of Astral, the company behind widely-used Python developer tools uv, Ruff, and ty — totaling hundreds of millions of downloads per month. The Astral team will join OpenAI's Codex team, which has seen 3x user growth and 5x usage increase since the start of 2026 with over 2 million weekly active users. OpenAI plans to continue supporting Astral's open source tools after the deal closes, while integrating the toolchain into the Codex software development workflow.
