OpenAI released native image generation capabilities within GPT-4o on March 25, 2025, enabling the model to generate and edit images directly in conversation without switching to a separate system. Unlike DALL-E 3, the 4o image generation is tightly integrated with the model's reasoning, allowing it to understand context, follow complex instructions, and use uploaded images as references. The feature went viral immediately for its ability to generate Studio Ghibli-style artwork and create coherent multi-panel scenes. It marked OpenAI's first truly multimodal generation model where vision understanding and image creation are unified.
