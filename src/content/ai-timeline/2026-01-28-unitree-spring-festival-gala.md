Unitree Robotics brought humanoid robots to China's CCTV Spring Festival Gala (春晚) — the most-watched TV event in the world with over 1 billion viewers. Their robots performed kung fu alongside martial arts students from Henan Tagou School, executing backflips and human-robot sparring sequences. The moment marked the mainstream cultural arrival of Chinese robotics and followed Unitree's announcement of 10,000+ unit annual production targets.
