Google launched NotebookLM on July 12, 2023, initially to a limited group of US users. Originally previewed at Google I/O 2023 as "Project Tailwind," NotebookLM is an AI-powered research and note-taking tool that grounds its responses in user-provided documents, reducing hallucination. The product became broadly available in December 2023, gained AI-generated audio podcast summaries ("Audio Overviews") in 2024, and quickly became one of Google's most praised AI consumer products. It represented a new paradigm for AI-assisted reading and research.
