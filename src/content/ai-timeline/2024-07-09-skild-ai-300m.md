Pittsburgh-based Skild AI emerged from stealth with a $300M Series A — the largest robotics AI round of 2024 — led by Lightspeed, Coatue, SoftBank, and Jeff Bezos. Skild is building the world's first general-purpose robotic foundation model: one brain that can control any robot across any task. At a $1.5B valuation, it signaled that the robotics AI race had entered a new capital-intensive phase.
