BMW's Leipzig plant launched a pilot program deploying AEON humanoid robots for automotive manufacturing tasks. The trial tested humanoids on real production lines for assembly assistance, part handling, and quality inspection — tasks previously requiring specialized machinery or human dexterity. BMW's entry into humanoid robot deployment validated the automotive industry's embrace of general-purpose robot workers as a near-term manufacturing reality.
