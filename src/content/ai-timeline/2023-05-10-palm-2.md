Google unveiled PaLM 2 at Google I/O on May 10, 2023, its next-generation large language model with significantly improved multilingual, reasoning, and coding capabilities. PaLM 2 powered over 25 Google products at launch, including Bard, Workspace AI features, and the new Med-PaLM 2 medical variant. It was available in four sizes (Gecko, Otter, Bison, Unicorn) allowing deployment across devices from mobile to enterprise scale. PaLM 2 represented Google's answer to GPT-4, demonstrating that training efficiency improvements could yield major capability gains without simply scaling parameters.
