Virtuals Protocol announced expansion to Solana, establishing the platform across both Base (Ethereum L2) and Solana ecosystems. The launch included a Meteora liquidity pool, a Strategic SOL Reserve (1% of trading fees converted to SOL), and new builder grants. Previously a Base-only AI agent launchpad that had reached $1B+ TVL, Virtuals' multi-chain expansion signaled the mainstreaming of AI agent tokens.
