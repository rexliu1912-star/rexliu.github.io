Hugging Face raised $235 million in a Series D funding round at a $4.5 billion valuation on August 23, 2023. The round was led by Salesforce Ventures and included investments from Google, Amazon, Nvidia, AMD, Intel, IBM, and Qualcomm — representing unprecedented backing from virtually all major tech companies. The investment validated Hugging Face's position as the central platform for open-source AI, hosting hundreds of thousands of models and datasets. The funding was earmarked for expanding the Hub, enterprise features, and compute infrastructure.
