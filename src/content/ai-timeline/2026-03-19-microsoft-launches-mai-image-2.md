Microsoft announced MAI-Image-2, its second-generation text-to-image model, debuting at #3 on the Arena.ai text-to-image leaderboard globally. The model features enhanced photorealism with accurate lighting and skin tones, reliable in-image text generation for posters and infographics, and rich scene generation for cinematic or surreal compositions. Available immediately in the MAI Playground.

微软发布 MAI-Image-2 文生图模型，在 Arena.ai 全球文生图排行榜中位列第三。新模型主打三大能力：增强的照片级写实效果（自然光影、准确肤色）、可靠的图内文字生成（适合海报、信息图）、以及复杂场景生成能力。现已在 MAI Playground 中开放试用。
