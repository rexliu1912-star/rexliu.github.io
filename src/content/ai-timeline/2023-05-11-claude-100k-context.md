Anthropic announced that Claude now supports a 100,000-token context window — equivalent to roughly 75,000 words or an entire book. This was a breakthrough in long-context AI, enabling users to analyze large codebases, lengthy documents, and complex research papers in a single prompt. The 100K context window set a new industry benchmark and significantly expanded Claude's practical use cases.
