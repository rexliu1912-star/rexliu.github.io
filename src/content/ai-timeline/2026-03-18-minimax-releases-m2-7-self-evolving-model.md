MiniMax released M2.7, its first model to participate in its own development through self-evolution. The model achieves 56.22% on SWE-Pro and 57.0% on Terminal Bench 2, approaching Claude Opus-level performance. M2.7 is capable of building complex agent harnesses, managing Agent Teams, and completing long-horizon productivity tasks. It maintains a 97% skill adherence rate with over 40 complex skills. Available via MiniMax Agent platform and API.
