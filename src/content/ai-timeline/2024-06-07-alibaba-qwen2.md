Alibaba releases Qwen2 — a family of models (0.5B to 72B) that tops open-source leaderboards for multilingual performance, with strong coding and reasoning. Qwen2-72B outperforms Llama 3 70B and challenges frontier closed models on Chinese benchmarks.

**So what?** Qwen2 set the benchmark for what a Chinese open-weight model family could be: multilingual by design (29 languages), strong on global benchmarks, and fully open-weight. For any enterprise needing Chinese + English AI without vendor lock-in, Qwen2 became the default. Alibaba's cloud distribution made it the most widely deployed open-source model in China.
