Google announced the merger of DeepMind and Google Brain into a single unit called Google DeepMind on April 14, 2023. The move consolidated Google's two most prominent AI research organizations under a unified leadership structure, with Demis Hassabis serving as CEO. The merger was designed to accelerate AI progress by combining DeepMind's foundational research strengths with Google Brain's deep expertise in large-scale machine learning. Google DeepMind became one of the most powerful AI research organizations in the world.
