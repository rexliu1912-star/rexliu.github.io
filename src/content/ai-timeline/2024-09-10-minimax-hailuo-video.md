MiniMax launches video-01 under its Hailuo AI brand — a high-quality text-to-video generation model that goes viral globally for its cinematic output quality, especially character consistency.

**So what?** While the West debated Sora's restricted access, China shipped. Hailuo AI's viral demos showed that video generation quality was no longer a US-exclusive capability. MiniMax proved it could build multimodal creative AI at consumer scale — and ship it openly to the public while OpenAI kept Sora behind waitlists.
