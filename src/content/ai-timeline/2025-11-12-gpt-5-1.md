OpenAI released GPT-5.1 on November 12, 2025, an upgrade to the GPT-5 series featuring warmer conversational tone, 8 customizable personality options, shopping research capabilities, and enhanced multimodal features. The model family launched in two primary modes: GPT-5.1 Instant for fast everyday tasks and GPT-5.1 Thinking for more complex reasoning. A week later, OpenAI added GPT-5.1-Codex-Max, an agentic coding model designed for extended autonomous software engineering tasks. GPT-5.1 marked OpenAI's first major upgrade cycle to GPT-5, refining the base model based on user feedback.
