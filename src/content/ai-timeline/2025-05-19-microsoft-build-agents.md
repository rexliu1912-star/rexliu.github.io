At Build 2025, Microsoft announces AI agents across Windows, GitHub, Azure, and Microsoft 365 — positioning the company around an "Agentic Web" where AI operates autonomously across enterprise workflows. GitHub Copilot crosses 180 million developer users.

**So what?** Microsoft is translating its OpenAI partnership into systemic OS-level advantage. When Copilot is in your IDE, your email, your browser, and your cloud — you don't switch tools, you switch modes. The enterprise lock-in flywheel is spinning at full speed.
