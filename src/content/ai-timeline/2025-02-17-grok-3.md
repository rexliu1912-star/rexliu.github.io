xAI released Grok-3 on February 17, 2025, trained on the 100,000-GPU Colossus supercluster with approximately 10x the computational resources of prior models. Grok-3 introduced "Think" mode, a chain-of-thought reasoning capability for complex problems, and "DeepSearch" for comprehensive research tasks. Benchmark results showed it competitive with or surpassing GPT-4.5 and Claude 3.7 Sonnet on math, science, and coding tasks. A premium SuperGrok subscription tier was introduced to access Grok-3's advanced features, with the model briefly made available to free X users as well.
