Anthropic released an upgraded Claude 3.5 Sonnet alongside the new Claude 3.5 Haiku, and introduced Computer Use — a groundbreaking capability allowing Claude to control a computer by viewing screenshots, moving the cursor, clicking, and typing. This marked the first time a widely available AI model could directly interact with a computer's graphical interface. Computer Use opened the door to autonomous agent workflows, though Anthropic cautioned it was an early, experimental feature. The update also included an improved Claude.ai interface.
