At Meta Connect 2023, Meta unveiled the Meta Quest 3 mixed-reality headset alongside a significant AI push. The event introduced AI Studio, enabling creators and businesses to build custom AI personas within Meta's social platforms. Meta also demonstrated AI-powered characters, real-time AI-generated backgrounds for video calls, and sticker generation in WhatsApp and Messenger. The announcements marked Meta's bet on the intersection of spatial computing and generative AI as the next computing platform.
