1X Technologies opened pre-orders for NEO, its home humanoid robot designed for everyday consumer use. Unlike industrial humanoids targeting factories, NEO is built for home environments — doing chores, assisting elderly residents, and learning household routines. Priced to reach mass market, NEO's launch represented the first serious attempt to bring a bipedal AI robot into the consumer home market.
