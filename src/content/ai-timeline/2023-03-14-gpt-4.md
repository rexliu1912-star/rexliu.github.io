OpenAI released GPT-4, its most capable large language model at the time, featuring multimodal inputs (text and images) and dramatically improved reasoning. GPT-4 scored in the top 10% of bar exam takers and significantly outperformed GPT-3.5 on standard benchmarks. The model powered ChatGPT Plus and was made available via API. GPT-4 set a new standard for frontier AI and accelerated enterprise adoption of large language models across industries.
