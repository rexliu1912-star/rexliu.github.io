Google launched Gemini 3 on November 18, 2025, its most capable AI model family to date, available from day one across Search, the Gemini app, AI Studio, Vertex AI, and the Gemini CLI. The flagship Gemini 3 Pro delivered state-of-the-art performance across reasoning, multimodal understanding, coding, and science benchmarks, with native support for text, images, audio, video, and code. Google also introduced Gemini 3 Deep Think, an enhanced reasoning mode pushing the limits of scientific and engineering problem-solving. The launch marked Google's most significant AI model release since Gemini 1.0, closing the perceived capability gap with OpenAI and Anthropic.
