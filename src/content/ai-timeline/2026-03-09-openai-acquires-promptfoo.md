OpenAI is acquiring Promptfoo, an AI security platform used by over 25% of Fortune 500 companies, with a widely-used open-source CLI for evaluating and red-teaming LLM applications. Once finalized, Promptfoo's capabilities will be integrated into OpenAI Frontier — the platform for building and operating AI coworkers — bringing native security testing, red-teaming, and oversight tooling to enterprise AI deployments.

**So what?** As agents get deployed into real workflows, security and eval infrastructure become table stakes. OpenAI buying Promptfoo signals they want to own that layer end-to-end inside Frontier rather than leaving it to third parties. The open-source project continues, but the enterprise roadmap now runs through OpenAI.
