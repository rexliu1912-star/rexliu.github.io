Google DeepMind and Isomorphic Labs unveiled AlphaFold 3 on May 8, 2024, a groundbreaking AI model capable of predicting the structure and interactions of all of life's molecules — proteins, DNA, RNA, ligands, and more. AlphaFold 3 dramatically improved upon its predecessor's accuracy, particularly for drug-target interactions. The model was made freely accessible to researchers via the AlphaFold Server. The release was hailed as a landmark in computational biology with profound implications for drug discovery and disease research.
