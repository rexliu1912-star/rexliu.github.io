At CES 2025, NVIDIA announced two major initiatives: Cosmos, a world foundation model platform for training physical AI and robotics systems using synthetic video data; and Project DIGITS, a $3,000 personal AI supercomputer delivering 1 petaflop of AI performance using the GB10 Grace Blackwell Superchip. Cosmos aimed to accelerate autonomous vehicle and robotics development by generating physically accurate simulation data. Project DIGITS brought AI supercomputer capability to a desktop form factor, democratizing local AI model deployment.
