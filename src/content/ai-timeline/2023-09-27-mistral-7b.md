Mistral AI released Mistral 7B on September 27, 2023, a 7-billion parameter open-source model that outperformed Meta's Llama 2 13B on every benchmark despite being half the size. Released via BitTorrent with an Apache 2.0 license with no usage restrictions, it became instantly available to the open-source community. Mistral 7B demonstrated that model efficiency could match brute-force scale, establishing Mistral as a leading open-source AI lab. The release was a watershed moment for small, highly capable language models.
