At the AI Seoul Summit, 16 leading AI companies including OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Meta, and xAI signed voluntary Frontier AI Safety Commitments. The commitments included publishing safety frameworks, conducting red-teaming, and sharing safety information with governments.
