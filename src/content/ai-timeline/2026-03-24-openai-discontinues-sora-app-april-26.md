OpenAI announced the discontinuation of Sora, its AI video generation app, with web and app experiences shutting down on April 26, 2026. The company stated it is reallocating compute resources toward other priorities, including world simulation for robotics. Sora had launched publicly in late 2024, just months before the shutdown announcement.

<!-- zh -->

OpenAI 宣布将于 2026 年 4 月 26 日正式停止 Sora 视频生成应用的 Web 和 App 服务。公司表示将把算力资源转向其他方向，包括服务于机器人领域的世界模拟技术。Sora 于 2024 年底正式向公众开放，运营仅约 6 个月。
