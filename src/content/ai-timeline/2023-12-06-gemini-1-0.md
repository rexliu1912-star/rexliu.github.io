Google unveiled Gemini 1.0 on December 6, 2023 — its most capable and general AI model, designed to be natively multimodal across text, images, audio, video, and code. Gemini came in three sizes: Ultra, Pro, and Nano. Gemini Ultra outperformed GPT-4 on 30 of 32 academic benchmarks and was the first model to surpass human experts on the MMLU benchmark. The launch marked Google's most significant response to ChatGPT and its renewed commitment to AI leadership.
