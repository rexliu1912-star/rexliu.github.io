Amazon announced a major strategic investment of up to $4 billion in Anthropic on September 25, 2023, with an initial $1.25 billion committed immediately. As part of the deal, Anthropic would use AWS as its primary cloud provider, and Amazon's customers would gain access to Claude models through Amazon Bedrock. Amazon also received a minority stake in Anthropic. The investment was Amazon's largest-ever venture bet and positioned AWS as a leading platform for enterprise AI — directly challenging Microsoft's OpenAI partnership.
