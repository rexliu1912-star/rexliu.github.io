Mistral released Mistral Small 4, a 119B-parameter Mixture-of-Experts model under the Apache 2.0 open-source license. The model unifies instruct, reasoning, vision, and coding capabilities in a single architecture (128 experts). It was announced alongside the Forge enterprise training platform at NVIDIA GTC and represents the next major release in the Mistral Small family.
