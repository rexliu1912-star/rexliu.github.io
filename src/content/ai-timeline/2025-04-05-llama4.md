Meta releases Llama 4 Scout (17B active / 109B total MoE) and Maverick, both natively multimodal. Also previews Llama 4 Behemoth — a teacher model claimed to outperform GPT-4.5 on STEM benchmarks.

**So what?** Meta bets on MoE + multimodal from day one instead of retrofitting vision into text-only models. With Scout deployable on a single H100 GPU, open-source closes the gap further. The Behemoth preview signals Meta's intent to compete at the very top — and give away the fruits publicly.
