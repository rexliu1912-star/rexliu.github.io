OpenAI released Sora 2 on September 30, 2025, a major upgrade to its video generation model featuring more physically accurate and realistic output, synchronized dialogue, and coherent sound effects. Sora 2 introduced a companion iOS app and significantly improved controllability over camera movements and scene composition. The model showed dramatic improvements in maintaining temporal consistency and generating complex multi-character interactions. Alongside the model launch, OpenAI introduced storyboard features allowing frame-by-frame creative control over generated videos.
