DeepSeek released DeepSeek-V2.5 in September 2024, merging the capabilities of DeepSeek-V2-Chat and DeepSeek-Coder-V2 into a unified model with improved general and coding abilities. The combined model delivered better alignment, stronger reasoning, and enhanced coding performance compared to running separate V2 variants. DeepSeek-V2.5 was subsequently revised in December 2024. The release demonstrated DeepSeek's rapid iteration pace in producing best-in-class open-source models.
