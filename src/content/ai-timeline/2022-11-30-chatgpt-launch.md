OpenAI launched ChatGPT on November 30, 2022, as a free research preview built on GPT-3.5. Within five days it attracted one million users, and within two months reached 100 million — the fastest consumer app growth in history. ChatGPT demonstrated that conversational AI could be genuinely useful for writing, coding, analysis, and Q&A at scale. The release triggered a wave of AI investment and competition that reshaped the entire technology industry.
