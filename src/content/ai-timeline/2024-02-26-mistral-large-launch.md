Mistral AI launched Mistral Large on February 26, 2024, its most capable model competing directly with GPT-4 and Claude 3. Mistral Large featured top-tier reasoning, 32K context window, strong multilingual abilities in English, French, Spanish, German, and Italian, and native function calling. It was made available through the La Plateforme API and Microsoft Azure. Mistral Large ranked second on the MMLU benchmark among publicly available models, firmly establishing Mistral as a frontier AI lab.
