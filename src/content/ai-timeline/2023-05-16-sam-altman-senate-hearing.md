OpenAI CEO Sam Altman testified before the US Senate Judiciary Subcommittee on Privacy, Technology, and the Law, calling for government regulation of AI. He suggested the creation of a new federal agency to license AI companies. The hearing marked AI's arrival as a mainstream political issue and set the stage for global regulatory debates.
