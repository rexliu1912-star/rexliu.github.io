Nvidia-backed British AI infrastructure startup Nscale has raised another megaround of $2 billion, lifting its valuation to $14.6B. Former Meta COO Sheryl Sandberg and former UK Deputy Prime Minister Nick Clegg have both joined its board.

**So what?** AI infrastructure is the arms race that won't slow down. Nscale landing two of tech/politics' most prominent names signals it's positioning as a serious European alternative to US hyperscalers — and the "Stargate Norway" framing is a deliberate play for sovereign AI infrastructure contracts.
