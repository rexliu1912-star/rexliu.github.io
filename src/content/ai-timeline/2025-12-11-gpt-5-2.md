OpenAI released GPT-5.2 on December 11, 2025, succeeding GPT-5.1 with a family of three models: GPT-5.2 Instant, GPT-5.2 Thinking, and GPT-5.2 Extended Thinking. The Extended Thinking variant allows the model to reason longer on difficult problems, making it competitive with specialized research-grade AI systems. GPT-5.2 introduced improved instruction following and better calibration on factual questions. The rapid succession from GPT-5.1 (released just a month prior) signaled OpenAI's accelerating iteration pace in the GPT-5 generation.
