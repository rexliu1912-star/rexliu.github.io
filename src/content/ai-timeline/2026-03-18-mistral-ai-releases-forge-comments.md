Mistral introduced Forge at NVIDIA GTC, a platform designed for enterprises and public sector organizations to build frontier-grade AI models grounded in their own proprietary data. Forge covers the full model lifecycle — creation, specialization, and continuous improvement — using Mistral's open-weight model library (including Mistral Small 4) without requiring customers to expose their data to the cloud.

<!-- zh -->

Mistral 在 NVIDIA GTC 上发布 Forge，一个面向企业和公共机构的 AI 模型训练平台。企业可以在自有数据基础上构建、微调和迭代模型，覆盖完整模型生命周期，数据无需上传至云端。基础模型库包含 Mistral Small 4 等开源权重模型。
