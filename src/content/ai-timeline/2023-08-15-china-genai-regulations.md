China's Cyberspace Administration enacted the "Interim Measures for the Management of Generative Artificial Intelligence Services," becoming one of the world's first comprehensive generative AI regulatory frameworks. The rules require AI services to align with "socialist core values," register with authorities, and label AI-generated content. A milestone in global AI governance.

中国网信办施行《生成式人工智能服务管理暂行办法》，成为全球首批综合性生成式 AI 监管框架之一。要求 AI 服务符合社会主义核心价值观、向主管部门备案、标注 AI 生成内容。全球 AI 治理的里程碑事件。
