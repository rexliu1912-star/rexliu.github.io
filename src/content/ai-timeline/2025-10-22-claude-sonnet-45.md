Anthropic released Claude Sonnet 4.5, bringing the hybrid reasoning capabilities first introduced in Opus 4.5 to the mid-tier model tier. Sonnet 4.5 offered improved coding performance, better instruction following, and enhanced multi-turn conversation quality at a lower cost than Opus. The release continued Anthropic's strategy of iterating rapidly across the full model family. Claude Sonnet 4.5 quickly became the most widely used Claude model due to its balance of capability and affordability.
