OpenAI completed its restructuring as a public benefit corporation (PBC) on October 28, 2025, after years of debate about its unusual nonprofit-controlled hybrid structure. The nonprofit entity was renamed the OpenAI Foundation and became a significant shareholder rather than the controlling entity. Microsoft received approximately 27% of OpenAI Group PBC under the new structure. The conversion cleared the path for a potential future IPO and aligned OpenAI's governance with its commercial scale, while maintaining its stated mission to develop AI for the benefit of humanity.
