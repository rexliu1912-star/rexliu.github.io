The 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry was split between **David Baker** (for computational protein design) and **Demis Hassabis & John Jumper** (for AlphaFold's protein structure prediction). AlphaFold solved a 50-year grand challenge in biology and has accelerated drug discovery globally. Two Nobel Prizes for AI in two consecutive days underscored the technology's transformative scientific impact.
