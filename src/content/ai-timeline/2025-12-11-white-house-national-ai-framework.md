The White House issued an executive order establishing a unified national AI policy framework, signaling federal preemption of fragmented state-level AI regulations. The order explicitly criticized state algorithmic discrimination laws and conditioned federal funding on AI policy alignment.

白宫签署行政令建立统一国家 AI 政策框架，释放联邦将优先于各州碎片化 AI 监管的信号。明确批评州级算法歧视法律，将联邦资金与 AI 政策对齐挂钩。
