Google DeepMind announced Gemini Robotics and Gemini Robotics-ER on March 12, 2025, vision-language-action (VLA) models built on Gemini 2.0 designed to give robots the ability to understand, reason about, and act in the physical world. Gemini Robotics can follow open-ended natural language instructions to perform complex manipulation tasks, while the ER (Embodied Reasoning) variant enables deeper spatial and physical understanding. The models demonstrated generalization across diverse robot morphologies and tasks without task-specific retraining. This marked Google DeepMind's first major foray into general-purpose robotics foundation models competitive with Physical Intelligence and Tesla Optimus.

Google DeepMind 发布 Gemini Robotics 和 Gemini Robotics-ER，基于 Gemini 2.0 的视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型，让机器人能理解、推理并在物理世界中行动。Gemini Robotics 可以遵循开放式自然语言指令执行复杂操作任务，ER 版本具备更深的空间和物理理解能力。无需针对特定任务重新训练即可泛化到不同机器人形态。这是 Google DeepMind 在通用机器人基础模型领域的首次重大布局。
