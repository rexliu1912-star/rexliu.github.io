xAI released Grok-2 on August 14, 2024, a major upgrade featuring multimodal vision understanding and access to Aurora, xAI's image generation model, making it one of the first commercial chatbots with integrated text-to-image generation. Grok-2 demonstrated competitive performance with GPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet on reasoning and knowledge benchmarks. It was made available to X Premium and Premium+ subscribers, with a more efficient Grok-2 mini variant released alongside it. The launch also marked xAI's first push toward making Grok a standalone product beyond just an X platform feature.
