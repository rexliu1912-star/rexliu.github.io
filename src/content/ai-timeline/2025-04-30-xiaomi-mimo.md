Xiaomi releases MiMo-7B, its first open-source reasoning model, which surpasses OpenAI o1-mini and QwQ-32B-Preview on AIME 2024-2025 and LiveCodeBench v5 — at 7B parameters.

**So what?** When a hardware company best known for affordable smartphones ships a best-in-class reasoning model, it signals AI has become table stakes for any serious tech player. Xiaomi's entry broadens China's AI competition beyond pure-play AI startups. With RMB 200B ($27.8B) in 5-year R&D commitments, hardware companies are becoming AI companies.
