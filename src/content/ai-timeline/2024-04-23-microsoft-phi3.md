Microsoft releases Phi-3-mini (3.8B), a small language model that achieves performance comparable to models 10× its size, capable of running fully on-device on a smartphone.

**So what?** AI isn't just about bigger and bigger — Phi-3 proved that quality data beats raw scale. For the first time, you could run a GPT-3.5 tier assistant locally without cloud. This kicked off the SLM race: on-device AI became viable, and every inference startup had to reckon with the edge. Microsoft's research team signaled: the future isn't only in the cloud.
