OpenAI released GPT-4.1 on April 14, 2025, a new model family in the API optimized for coding, instruction following, and long-context tasks with a 1 million token context window. GPT-4.1 outperformed GPT-4.5 Preview on many key benchmarks while being significantly cheaper and faster, leading to the deprecation of GPT-4.5 Preview. It came in three sizes — GPT-4.1, GPT-4.1 mini, and GPT-4.1 nano — serving different cost-performance tradeoffs. GPT-4.1 quickly became the default model for developers building production applications on the OpenAI API.
