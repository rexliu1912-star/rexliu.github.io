Meta released Llama 3.2 at Meta Connect on September 25, 2024, introducing multimodal vision capabilities to the Llama model family for the first time. The release included 11B and 90B vision-enabled models alongside lightweight 1B and 3B models optimized for edge and mobile deployment. Llama 3.2 could process both text and images, enabling tasks like visual question answering and document analysis. The lightweight models were designed to run on-device, including on Meta's smart glasses and smartphones.
