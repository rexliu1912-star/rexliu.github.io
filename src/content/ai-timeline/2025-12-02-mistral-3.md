Mistral releases Mistral Large 3 (41B active / 675B total sparse MoE) and Ministral 3 family (3B, 7B, 14B) — small enough to run on laptops and drones with 4GB VRAM using 4-bit quantization.

**So what?** Mistral is positioning itself as the "runs anywhere" open AI stack: from frontier server inference down to embedded edge devices. As enterprise AI moves to on-premise and edge-first deployments, having a credible open model across the full compute spectrum is a moat no US-closed lab can match.
