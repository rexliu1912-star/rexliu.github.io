Tesla began deploying Optimus humanoid robots internally across its manufacturing facilities for autonomous factory work. With thousands of units in Fremont's pilot production line and Elon Musk projecting 1 million Optimus units per year at full scale, this marked the first time a major automaker deployed general-purpose humanoid robots for real production tasks. Tesla simultaneously announced Gen 3 design improvements and aggressive 2025-2026 production targets.
