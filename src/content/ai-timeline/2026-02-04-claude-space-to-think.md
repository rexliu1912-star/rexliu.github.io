Anthropic published a statement committing to keeping Claude.ai an ad-free, distraction-free environment focused on genuine helpfulness. The announcement differentiated Claude from ad-supported AI services by emphasizing user trust and privacy. Anthropic articulated its philosophy that AI assistants should serve users' thinking rather than monetizing their attention. This positioning reinforced Anthropic's mission-driven approach to AI deployment distinct from advertising-dependent platforms.
