NEAR Protocol announced a strategic pivot to become the chain for user-owned AI, launching NEAR AI with plans for AI assistants, agent infrastructure, and decentralized AI model training. Co-founder Illia Polosukhin (Transformer paper co-author) positioned NEAR as the natural bridge between AI and crypto.
