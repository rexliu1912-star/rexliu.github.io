NEAR Protocol formalized its strategic pivot to become the AI layer for Web3, emphasizing chain abstraction and AI-native decentralized applications. The foundation announced a $20M fund to accelerate AI development on NEAR. The strategy repositioned NEAR from a general L1 blockchain to a specialized platform for building AI agents and autonomous on-chain applications.
