Hugging Face surpasses 1 million public AI model listings on its Hub, cementing its role as the de facto repository of open-source AI. Growing from 300K models in March 2024 to 1M in September.

**So what?** GitHub moment for AI. When a platform has 1M models, it stops being a tool and becomes infrastructure — every company building AI touches Hugging Face. The exponential growth reflects open-source AI's commoditization: what required frontier labs in 2023 is now community-built and freely shared. Anyone building closed systems is fighting an increasingly large open tide.
