OpenAI launched Canvas, a new collaborative interface within ChatGPT designed for writing and coding projects that go beyond simple chat. Canvas opens in a separate window alongside the chat, giving users a persistent workspace where they can make targeted edits, run Python code, and iterate on documents or code with AI assistance. The feature was initially rolled out to ChatGPT Plus and Team users before becoming available to all users in December 2024. Canvas represented OpenAI's push to make ChatGPT a full-fledged creative and development co-pilot.
