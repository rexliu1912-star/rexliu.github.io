OpenAI launched the GPT Store, a marketplace where users can discover and deploy custom GPTs built by creators worldwide. At launch, over 3 million custom GPTs had already been created by users since the feature was announced at DevDay in November 2023. The store featured categories including productivity, education, and programming. OpenAI also announced plans for a revenue-sharing program with top GPT creators, signaling a new creator economy around custom AI assistants.
