Microsoft announced a multiyear, multi-billion-dollar investment in OpenAI on January 23, 2023 — reported to total $10 billion over multiple years. The deal extended and deepened the partnership that began in 2019, giving Microsoft exclusive rights to commercialize OpenAI's technologies through Azure. Microsoft planned to integrate OpenAI models across its product suite including Office 365, Bing, and GitHub Copilot. The investment transformed Microsoft's competitive position versus Google and was the defining AI partnership deal of the era.
