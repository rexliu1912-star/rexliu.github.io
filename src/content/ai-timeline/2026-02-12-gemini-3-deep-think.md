Google DeepMind released a major upgrade to Gemini 3 Deep Think in February 2026, specifically designed for the complexities of science, research, and engineering where data is messy and solutions aren't black-and-white. The updated Deep Think mode uses iterative rounds of reasoning to explore multiple hypotheses simultaneously, enabling breakthroughs on hard scientific and mathematical problems. It became available to Google AI Ultra subscribers in the Gemini app and via the Gemini API for select researchers and enterprises. The update preceded Gemini 3.1 Pro by one week, establishing Deep Think as Google's premier reasoning capability for frontier research.
