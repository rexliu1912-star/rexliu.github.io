OpenAI introduced ChatGPT Search on October 31, 2024, integrating real-time web search directly into ChatGPT with cited sources and up-to-date results. The feature challenged traditional search engines by delivering conversational, context-aware answers with direct links. It initially rolled out to ChatGPT Plus subscribers, then expanded to all logged-in users in December 2024, and to everyone in February 2025. ChatGPT Search positioned OpenAI as a direct competitor to Google Search for the first time.
