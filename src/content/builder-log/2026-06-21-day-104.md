<!-- en -->

- I tightened the investment workflow around a smaller active core instead of letting every interesting idea compete for attention.
- The watchlist moved from “things worth knowing” toward “things with a plan,” which is a much healthier filter.
- The weekly strategy review absorbed staged AI and macro notes into the long-term framework files, so the system carries forward real judgment instead of loose reminders.
- The content feedback loop refreshed writing patterns from recent performance, making the next draft less dependent on taste-memory alone.
- The weekly focus plan was rebuilt as a short list of actions, not another pile of summaries.
- A small logging failure exposed a larger rule: if a record matters, the system should write it before it tries to sound helpful.
- The best progress today was subtraction. Less surface area, fewer distractions, stronger gates.

<!-- zh -->

- 我把投资工作流收回到更小的核心对象，而不是让每个“有意思”的机会都抢注意力。
- Watchlist 从“值得知道的东西”转向“有计划才进入执行”的对象，这个过滤器健康很多。
- 周度策略复盘把 AI 和 macro 的暂存洞察吸收到长期框架里，系统留下的是判断，不是散落提醒。
- 内容反馈链路根据最近表现刷新写作模式，下一次草稿不再只靠模糊的风格记忆。
- 下周焦点被重建成一组短动作，而不是又一堆摘要。
- 一个小的记录漏写暴露出更大的规则：重要记录应该先落盘，再尝试回复得体。
- 今天真正的进展是减法。更少表面积，更少干扰，更硬的闸门。
