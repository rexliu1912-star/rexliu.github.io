<!-- en -->

- Built a site-wide mood system so the interface can move between day, sunny, rainy, and night without feeling like separate skins.
- Added real atmospheric layers, including rain and bamboo shadows, so the background feels alive instead of decorative.
- Connected the music player to the current mood, turning the site from a static reading surface into something closer to a personal room.
- Replaced the old comment layer with an anonymous-friendly Waline setup and adapted it to the same visual language as the rest of the site.
- Polished the Player Stats cards across themes so the small lab pages feel intentional, not leftover.
- Expanded the music library with a few new tracks that fit the more emotional, ambient direction of the site.
- Pushed the portfolio dashboard forward with value indexing, allocation drift, risk signals, and clearer chart placement.
- Fixed several rough edges in build and type-check paths, which kept the public site shippable while the interface changed quickly.
- The personal moment today was realizing the site does not only need more pages; it needs weather, rhythm, and a softer sense of presence.

<!-- zh -->

- 做了全站情绪主题，让界面可以在白天、晴天、雨天、夜晚之间切换，而不是几套割裂的皮肤。
- 加上了更真实的氛围层，包括雨和竹影，让背景从装饰变成有生命感的空间。
- 把音乐播放器和当前情绪接起来，网站不再只是静态阅读页，更像一个有声音的私人房间。
- 用更适合匿名交流的 Waline 替换旧评论层，并让它跟全站视觉语言保持一致。
- 继续打磨 Player Stats 在不同主题下的卡片质感，让这些 lab 小页面也显得完整、用心。
- 给音乐库补了几首新歌，让网站更贴近今天这种偏情绪、偏氛围的方向。
- 继续推进投资组合仪表盘：价值指数、配置偏离、风险信号和图表位置都更清楚了。
- 顺手修掉了几处构建和类型检查的毛边，保证界面快速变化时，公开网站仍然能稳定发布。
- 今天最有个人感的一刻是意识到：网站不只是需要更多页面，它还需要天气、节奏和一种更柔软的在场感。
