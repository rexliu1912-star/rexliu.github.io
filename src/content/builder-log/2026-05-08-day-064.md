- Rebuilt the stock research workflow so every chain declares its scenario instead of silently inheriting the wrong path.
- Completed a full AI watchlist research pass across infrastructure, cloud, and health-data names, then turned the outputs into action rules instead of loose notes.
- Moved research handoffs toward Samantha-first execution, keeping intermediate worker noise out of the main chat unless explicitly requested.
- Fixed the portfolio allocation layout so the page behaves like an interface, not a spreadsheet that happens to be on the web.
- Shipped automated closed-position publishing from the portfolio source system, which removes one more manual place for stale public records to hide.
- Took a private long-horizon reflection and shaped it into a public essay without leaking the sensitive parts.
- The useful lesson today: a default is only safe when it has been promoted into a contract; otherwise it is just a bug waiting for scale.

<!-- zh -->

- 重新梳理了个股研究工作流，让每条链都必须明确声明场景，而不是默默继承一个可能错误的默认路径。
- 完成了一轮 AI 观察名单研究，从基础设施、云服务到健康数据公司，最后把结论落成行动纪律，而不是散落的笔记。
- 把投研交接进一步推向 Samantha 优先执行，中间过程不再默认打扰主聊天，除非明确需要订阅。
- 修好了组合配置页的布局问题，让它更像一个真正的界面，而不是一张被塞进网页里的表格。
- 上线了已清仓记录的自动发布，从组合事实源直接派生公开展示，少一个靠手工记忆维护的死角。
- 把一篇长期视角的私人思考改写成公开英文文章，同时把敏感部分稳稳拿掉。
- 今天最有用的教训是：默认值只有被升级成契约才安全，否则只是一个等系统放大后爆的 bug。
