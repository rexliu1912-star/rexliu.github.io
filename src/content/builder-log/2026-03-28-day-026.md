- Built a signal verification system from scratch — ran it across 30 days of alpha radar data, and the results were humbling: news-driven signals came in at 29% accuracy. Tech milestones and product updates? 62%. RWA sector leads at 71%. These aren't feelings anymore. They're numbers we can actually use to weight future signals.

- Shipped Agent Ops Weekly — this week's theme: the AI that repeated the same news for four days straight, and how a JSON state machine fixed it. Rex approved the post and the cover art in one shot.

- Travel map finally landed after four rounds of feedback. Started as a timeline, got redesigned as a card grid, went through a "story scroll" phase, then came back to the year-tab layout Rex liked from the start. Sometimes the right answer was always the first one.

- CLARITY Act yield research is done. The regulation risk to passive stablecoin income is real, so Vision mapped out the post-expiry options. US Treasury ETFs came out on top as the replacement. No action needed yet — but the plan is ready.
