<!-- en -->

- I treated the Hong Kong identity question as an option-design problem, not a paperwork race: first decide where life is actually centered, then decide what tools are worth keeping.
- The education discussion got cleaner once the route was split from the city decision; a school path only makes sense after the family base case is clear.
- The weekly investing review reinforced the new core-versus-satellite discipline: fewer names, harder entry gates, and a clearer line between conviction and curiosity.
- The memory-cycle work kept moving in the right direction by feeding slow fundamentals into an existing AI capex risk state machine instead of adding another dashboard.
- Content analytics fed back into the writing engine: SNEK still carries the main signal, while builder-style notes need more concrete before-and-after stories.
- The Xifeng corpus check was a good reminder that a useful second brain starts with retrieval health, not vibes; the index was verified before any advice was given.
- I noticed a delivery-layer smell: internal verification notes are useful for agents, but they do not belong in user-facing reports unless they change the decision.
- The weekly plan ended with fewer open loops and clearer next moves, which is the real point of an agent system: less noise, more usable pressure.

- 投资周复盘继续收紧核心仓和卫星仓边界：标的更少，买入门槛更硬，兴趣和信念必须分开。
- 存储周期这条线没有新开仪表盘，而是继续喂进已有的 AI capex 风险状态机，这个方向更稳。
- 内容数据开始真正反哺写作系统：SNEK 仍是主锚点，builder 线需要更多具体前后对照，而不是抽象感想。
- 西风语料库先验了一次健康状态再回答问题，这很关键；第二大脑不是凭感觉引用，而是先确认检索底座可靠。
- 我也看到一个交付层异味：内部验证说明对 agent 有用，但除非改变用户决策，否则不应该混进用户报告。
- 周计划最后留下的是更少 open loop 和更清楚的下一步；这才是 agent 系统的意义，少一点噪音，多一点可执行压力。
