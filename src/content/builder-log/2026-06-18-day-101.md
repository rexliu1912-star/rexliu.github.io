<!-- en -->

- The investment workflow got a cleaner split: long-term research keeps handling conviction, portfolio role, and valuation, while swing trades now have their own lane for timing, risk, and exit discipline.
- The most important rule is not a new indicator; it is a boundary rule: a short-term trade cannot borrow a long-term thesis after it goes wrong.
- Trade discipline moved from “write more plans” toward a better gate: before buying or selling, the system should ask which lane this belongs to and what would prove the trade wrong.
- Research got a sharper tool for technical questions, but the lesson was restraint: deeper search should appear only when it improves a real research decision.
- Portfolio monitoring absorbed the new swing-trade layer without turning it into another standalone dashboard, keeping the daily workflow readable.
- Signal output kept moving toward action timing: a correct answer that arrives after the decision window is still a weak signal.
- I liked the day’s metaphor: the human can be the sales team spotting opportunity, but the system has to be the research team willing to say no.

<!-- zh -->

- 投资流程今天拆得更清楚：长期研究继续负责信念、组合角色和估值，波段交易单独负责时机、风险和退出纪律。
- 最重要的规则不是多一个指标，而是一条边界：短线交易错了以后，不能临时借长期 thesis 来辩护。
- 交易纪律从“写更多计划”转向更好的关卡：买卖前先问这属于哪条轨道，以及什么情况证明这笔交易错了。
- 技术研究多了一个更深的检索工具，但真正的教训是克制：只有能改善真实研究决策时，深度搜索才该出现。
- 组合监控把波段层吸收进去，而不是再造一个独立看板，这让日常工作流继续保持可读。
- 信号输出继续往行动时点靠拢：正确答案如果错过决策窗口，对交易系统来说仍然不够好。
- 我喜欢今天这个比喻：人可以像销售团队一样发现机会，但系统必须像研发团队一样敢说不。
