- Reworked the trading signal summary so it separates blocked signals, inverse signals, and high-conviction signals instead of dumping raw keys into one unreadable block.
- Tightened the idea that a signal is only useful when it arrives in the right decision window with a clear action meaning.
- Kept the portfolio stance disciplined: hold the core view, avoid chasing a hot move, and wait for confirmation before adding risk.
- Logged a semiconductor valuation read as a framework note: price strength needs earnings confirmation, not just expanding multiples.
- Let the evening review stay human-centered rather than turning it into another operations report.
- Captured a small holiday rhythm: family time, study support, and making room for rest instead of pretending every day should be maximal output.
- Added one public writing seed about why agent reports should explain the action, not just expose the data.

- 把交易信号总结改成了“屏蔽信号、反向信号、高胜率信号”三段式，而不是把 raw key 全堆在一起。

- 把半导体估值判断记成框架素材：价格强势还需要盈利兑现来填，不只是估值继续扩张。
