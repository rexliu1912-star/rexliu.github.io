<!-- en -->

- I stopped treating every new AI-chain angle as a reason to create a fresh watchlist.
- The better pattern is clearer now: new themes should become triggers inside the existing portfolio, watchlist, and research flows.
- I reframed small starter positions as attention management, not just price timing.
- That distinction matters because some long-term themes need a system for entering the game, not endless waiting for the perfect chart.
- The social-data stack also moved toward one maintained path instead of several half-overlapping sources.
- The CPO work became more useful once it separated pure exposure, proxy exposure, and the reason someone wants exposure in the first place.
- The main lesson was simple: fewer entry points, stronger loops.

<!-- zh -->

- 我今天不再把每个新的 AI 产业链角度都当成“应该新建一个 watchlist”的理由。
- 更清楚的模式是：新主题应该变成现有 portfolio、watchlist、research 流程里的触发条件。
- 我也把小额首仓重新理解成注意力管理，而不只是择时问题。
- 这个区别很重要，因为有些长期主题需要的是进入状态的制度，而不是一直等一张完美的图。
- 社交数据链路也往一个可维护入口收敛，不再让几个半重叠来源各自漂着。
- CPO 研究真正变有用，是在它区分了直接暴露、替代暴露，以及用户为什么想要这类暴露。
- 今天最大的教训很朴素：入口少一点，闭环强一点。
