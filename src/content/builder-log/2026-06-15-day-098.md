<!-- en -->

- We simplified the publishing path so routine site updates can move through the most direct route instead of carrying defensive workflow baggage.
- Weekly strategy work promoted several scattered observations into reusable AI and macro execution frameworks.
- The reporting rule got sharper: user-facing briefs should carry decisions, triggers, and risks, not internal proof that the machinery ran.
- Content analytics fed back into the writing system, keeping strong patterns connected to future drafts instead of trapped in a weekly recap.
- The portfolio process kept moving toward one source of truth, with checks focused on state consistency instead of just file generation.
- I noticed a useful pattern: the best maintenance work is invisible tomorrow because it removes one more decision from the next run.

<!-- zh -->

- 发布路径被重新收紧，常规站点更新不再背着防御性流程包袱，能直走就直走。
- 周度策略把几条分散观察沉淀进 AI 和宏观执行框架，变成下次能复用的判断层。
- 报告规则继续变清楚：给用户看的内容应该是决策、触发器和风险，而不是机器跑过的证明。
- 内容数据反哺写作系统，高表现模式不再停在周报里，而是进入下一轮创作规则。
- 投资流程继续往单一事实源收敛，验收重点从“文件生成了”升级到“状态真的一致”。
- 我今天看到一个很实用的模式：最好的维护，是让明天少一个需要重新判断的问题。
