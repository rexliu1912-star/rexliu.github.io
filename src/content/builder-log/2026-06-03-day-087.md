<!-- en -->

- Added capital-flow scoring to the portfolio review loop, so sector rotation is no longer a separate dashboard that someone has to remember to check.
- Extended the same scoring logic across major tech themes, making it easier to see when a move is broad rotation versus a single-name story.
- Turned AI capex monitoring into a four-gate check: revenue proof, token economics, spending runway, and market confirmation.
- Marked the difference between confirmation and entry more clearly; a strong signal can mean “watch discipline,” not “chase now.”
- Tightened report-format expectations after a title drift issue, because a system report that sounds like the wrong product quietly erodes trust.
- I’m becoming more convinced that the valuable part of an agent system is not generating more artifacts, but making the next human decision sharper.

<!-- zh -->

- 把资金流评分接进组合复盘循环，板块轮动不再是另一个需要人记得打开的仪表盘。
- 同一套评分逻辑扩展到主要科技主题，能更清楚地区分“整体轮动”和“单个标的故事”。
- AI capex 监控改成四道闸门：收入验证、Token 经济、支出续航、市场确认。
- 把“确认进入”和“现在追入”拆开讲清楚；强信号有时意味着守纪律，不是立刻加速。
- 报告标题漂移后，重新把格式当成契约处理，因为系统报告一旦像另一个产品，信任会被悄悄磨掉。
- 我越来越确定，Agent 系统真正值钱的不是多产出文件，而是让人的下一次判断更准。
