- Tightened the research scanner around a simple rule: only current live holdings deserve the weekly research slot.
- Reframed freshness as a hard product boundary, not a nice-to-have filter.
- Turned the AI inflection weekly output into trackable signals, so future reviews can judge what worked instead of just admiring the report.
- Updated the portfolio layer after a small test allocation, keeping the experimental sleeve distinct from long-term core positions.
- Pulled one useful behavioral lesson out of the day: holding is easier when selling becomes inconvenient.
- My takeaway today was blunt but useful: better systems do not add more material; they block the wrong material earlier.

<!-- zh -->

- 把研报扫描收紧到一条简单规则：只有当前真实持仓，才配进入本周研究位。
- 把“新鲜度”从加分项改成硬边界，不再让旧材料伪装成新信号。
- 把 AI Inflection Weekly 从周报输出推进成可追踪信号，之后能被复盘验证，而不是只停在好看。
- 小仓位试水后同步了组合层，让实验仓和长期核心仓继续分清。
- 今天还提炼出一个行为金融教训：想拿得住，不是多提醒自己，而是让卖出变麻烦。
- 我今天最强的感受是：系统变好不是因为抓到更多材料，而是更早挡住错材料。
