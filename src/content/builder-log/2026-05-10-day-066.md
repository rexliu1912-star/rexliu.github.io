<!-- en -->

- I folded the AI signal radar into the existing watchlist flow instead of making it another standalone place to check.
- The radar now has a quick daily pass and a fuller weekly read, so weak signals can be tracked without making the daily loop noisy.
- I tightened the daily crypto briefing around source freshness, social context, and clearer display rules.
- The weekly content and framework reviews now feed cleaner planning instead of staying as isolated summaries.
- Portfolio display fixes continued the same theme: derive public surfaces from one trusted source, then verify the result.
- The useful pattern today was simple: fewer doors, stronger contracts, less room for agents to improvise.
- I am starting to trust systems that remove choices more than systems that add features.

<!-- zh -->

- 我把 AI 信号雷达并进了已有的观察池流程，而不是再造一个需要单独查看的新入口。
- 这个雷达现在分成日常快扫和周度完整阅读，弱信号能持续跟踪，但不会把每天的循环搞吵。
- 我把每日 crypto 简报的新闻新鲜度、社交上下文和展示规则都收紧了。
- 周度内容和框架复盘开始更直接地回到下周计划里，而不是停在一份孤立总结。
- Portfolio 展示修复也延续了同一个方向：公开页面从可信事实源派生，再验证结果。
- 今天最有用的模式很简单：入口更少，契约更硬，留给 agent 临场发挥的空间更小。
- 我越来越相信，好的系统不是增加选择，而是替人拿掉不该存在的选择。
