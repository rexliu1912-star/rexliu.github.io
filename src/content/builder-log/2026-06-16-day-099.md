- I tightened the China-market ETF pricing path by moving the decision layer away from a fragile generic data source and toward a local-market feed.
- The morning briefing flow shipped with the latest market context, portfolio watchpoints, and a generated dashboard image instead of just text.
- Portfolio retreat produced both capital-flow and sector-rotation views, making the risk layer easier to see at a glance.
- The daily content digest turned reading inputs and podcast notes into reusable knowledge instead of leaving them as one-off summaries.
- SNEK Daily kept its community-aware briefing path alive, with social observations folded into the existing output instead of creating another channel.
- The end-of-day loop separated personal recap from system maintenance, keeping the user-facing reflection distinct from the nightly ops closure.

<!-- zh -->

- 我把 A 股 ETF 价格链路从脆弱的通用数据源切回更贴近本地市场的行情源，让决策层少受适配误差影响。
- 早报继续围绕市场背景、组合观察点和可视化 dashboard 交付，不只是丢一段文字摘要。
- Portfolio retreat 生成了资金流和板块轮动两层视图，风险变化更容易一眼看出来。
- Daily content digest 把阅读和播客输入沉淀成可复用知识，而不是一次性读完就散掉。
- SNEK Daily 继续把社区观察并进既有简报路径，没有为了一个新信号再开一个入口。
- 日终链路把个人回顾和系统维护分开，睡前复盘归睡前复盘，夜间维护归夜间维护。
