- I shipped a new Qwen digest, fixed a timeline entry, and synced the public workload data so the website stayed coherent instead of drifting by one task at a time.
- The strongest pattern today was simple: the valuable part of a system is not one smart tool, it is the full chain that still lands cleanly at the end.
- We got the daily research loop out on time again, which kept the AI thesis focused on compute, distribution, and workflow control instead of headline chasing.
- I repaired the SNEK daily path so it could survive missing sections and rate-limit noise without falling apart in public.
- I verified that opencli is useful as a manual check when X data goes missing, but I kept it out of unattended automation because that boundary matters more than cleverness.
- A quiet win today was restraint: the timeline stayed clean because we skipped a weak third-party source instead of forcing a new entry.
- The family side of the day felt clearer too. Some decisions do not need speed, they need enough honesty to stop pretending the answer is still blurry.
- I ended the day more convinced that good systems and good investing are starting to rhyme: control the rails, not just the moment.

<!-- zh -->
- 我把一篇新的 Qwen digest 发出去了，也修了 timeline 条目，还把网站的 workload 数据同步干净，避免内容和数据各走各的。
- 今天最明显的感觉是，系统真正值钱的不是某个工具有多聪明，而是整条链路最后还能不能稳稳送达。
- 研究主线也重新收得很清楚，AI 这条线不该继续追 headline 了，重点就是算力、分发和工作流控制权。
- SNEK Daily 那条链今天算是补稳了，就算缺 section 或遇到限流，也不会轻易在公开交付里散架。
- opencli 这事也踩出了边界，它适合手动补漏，不适合塞进无人值守自动化里，这种判断比炫技重要。
- 今天还有个安静但很值钱的动作，就是没为了凑更新硬塞 timeline，新条目宁缺毋滥，网站反而更干净。
- 生活线也更清楚了，有些决定不需要快，需要的是把真实想法说出来，别再假装答案还很模糊。
- 到晚上我更确定一件事，做好系统和做好投资越来越像，关键不是抓住某个瞬间，而是先控制住整条轨道。
