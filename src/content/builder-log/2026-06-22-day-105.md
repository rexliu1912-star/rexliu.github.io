<!-- en -->

- Folded an AI inflection report back into the research scan so technical signals show up beside holdings, papers, and watchlist context instead of as another inbox.
- Rebuilt the investment research shelf around sectors rather than monthly folders, which makes the system feel less like an archive and more like a living map.
- Added a stronger rule for research notes: every company file needs a visible research date so old conviction cannot pretend to be fresh judgment.
- Tuned trade alerts so only signals with historical edge reach the main channel, while lower-value event noise gets absorbed into the morning briefing.
- Caught a portfolio-sync false green light: the script said everything was done, but the facts still needed a read-back from trades and positions.
- Rotated a semiconductor expression from one-company exposure into a broader memory-cycle ETF, keeping the same theme but changing the risk shape.
- Logged a small family-learning moment too, because the quiet records matter as much as the loud dashboards.

<!-- zh -->

- 把独立的 AI 拐点周报收回 research-scan，让技术信号和持仓、论文、watchlist 放在同一个研究窗口里，而不是多一个收件箱。
- 把投资研究目录从按月份归档改成按赛道组织，系统不再像仓库，更像一张还会继续生长的地图。
- 给公司研报加上更硬的规则：每份文件都要看得见调研时间，旧判断不能伪装成新判断。
- 收紧交易提醒：只有历史胜率过线的信号进主频道，低价值事件噪音并入早报。
- 抓到一次 portfolio sync 的假绿灯：脚本说完成，但事实层还必须读回 trades 和 positions 才算数。
- 把半导体表达从单公司风险切到更宽的内存周期 ETF，同一条主题，但风险形状变了。
- 也记下了一个很小的家庭学习瞬间，因为安静的记录不该输给响亮的 dashboard。
