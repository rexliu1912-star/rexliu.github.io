<!-- en -->
- Upgraded OpenClaw and fixed Telegram media handling, so the daily reporting loop stayed reliable instead of half-working in the background.

- Moved the whole agent stack off Anthropic and onto a new model setup, turning a cost shock into a cleaner system reset.

- Kept the briefing pipeline alive through the transition, with the evening report, weekly review, and daily digest all landing on time.

- Confirmed that model evaluation still works best on real tasks, not leaderboards — a translation review told me more than any benchmark page could.

- Saw a clearer signal from the content system: series-based writing and data-backed posts still outperform empty opinion drops.

- Let Vision stay conservative in a fear-heavy market instead of forcing a hot take, which matters more than sounding smart for one day.

- Used the nightly review to catch a quieter problem too: some runbooks are still producing activity without enough hit rate, so next week needs sharper pruning.

<!-- zh -->
- 今天先把 OpenClaw 升级了，也顺手把 Telegram 媒体链路修顺，不然很多日报其实都只是“勉强能跑”。

- 整套 Agent 系统开始彻底去 Anthropic 化，这次不是小修小补，是借着成本变化把模型分工重新梳了一遍。

- 一边换底层，一边还把晚报、周更和 digest 这些主流程都稳稳跑完，节奏没断。

- 也再次确认了一件事：测模型最有用的方式，还是拿真实工作直接上，不是去看排行榜。

- 内容系统今天给了更清楚的反馈——系列化内容、带数据支撑的内容，还是比空观点短推更能打。

- Vision 在极度恐慌的市场里没有硬凑观点，这种克制比“说得很满”更值钱。

- 晚上的复盘还顺手抓到一个更深的问题：有些 runbook 看起来很忙，但命中率不够，下周该砍的还是得砍。
