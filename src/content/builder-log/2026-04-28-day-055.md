<!-- en -->

- I spent the day turning the portfolio page from a data-heavy screen into a clearer decision surface.
- The portfolio structure was simplified so the most important context appears earlier and repeated sections no longer compete for attention.
- The action area gained a more explicit signal layer, making it easier to separate observation from next-step thinking.
- Collapsible sections became smoother and more useful, with header-click behavior, section summaries, and clearer active-state feedback.
- The crypto management copy and navigation labels became bilingual, so the page now feels more consistent with the rest of the site.
- The broader site picked up a softer light-theme atmosphere through purple-blue gradients, warm gold accents, and dappled sunlight details.
- The header and navigation were tuned to sit naturally on gradient pages instead of feeling like a separate strip.
- Small cleanup passes removed unused code and kept the implementation from carrying old design leftovers.

<!-- zh -->

- 我今天把投资组合页从“信息很多的面板”，往“更容易做判断的界面”推进了一步。
- 投资组合的信息结构被重新梳理，重要上下文更靠前，重复模块也不再互相抢注意力。
- Action 区域增加了更清晰的信号层，让“观察到什么”和“接下来怎么想”更容易分开。
- 折叠区交互变得更顺，支持点击标题、展示小结，也有更明确的当前状态反馈。
- Crypto management 的文案和导航标签补齐了双语，和站点其他页面的气质更统一。
- 整个站点的浅色主题更柔和了：紫蓝渐变、暖金色光感和树影细节一起把氛围拉出来。
- Header 和导航也跟渐变页面重新对齐，不再像一条单独贴上去的横条。
- 顺手清掉了一些无用代码和旧设计残留，让实现继续保持轻一点。
