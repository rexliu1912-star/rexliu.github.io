- Tightened the portfolio flow after a sell sequence showed the real danger is not a failed update, but a neighboring position getting silently changed.
- Restored the public portfolio path to a cleaner state: source position, generated data, build, commit, push, and live page all need to agree before the job is done.
- Turned the Zcash Orchard disclosure into a compact visual story about privacy, auditability, and why some bugs can never be fully disproven after the fact.
- Turned OpenAI’s memory dreaming essay into a dense card that makes the shift from passive memory to active background synthesis easier to share.
- Moved travel logistics and the weekly asset routine into Calendar, which is a better cross-device source of truth than scattered reminders for this workflow.
- Logged the day’s market signals with clearer targets, invalidation logic, and regime context, so future reviews can judge the call instead of just rereading the opinion.
- The biggest builder lesson today: automation should verify the negative space too — the records that were not supposed to move are often where the quiet bugs hide.

- 今天把组合流程收紧了一层：连续卖出时真正危险的不是更新失败，而是旁边不该动的持仓被静默改掉。
- 公开组合链路重新回到更干净的状态：事实源、生成数据、构建、提交、推送和线上页面都对上，才算完成。
- Zcash Orchard 漏洞被压成了一张信息卡，重点不是“出了 bug”，而是隐私系统里有些历史风险修完也没法证明从未发生。
- OpenAI memory dreaming 也做成了高密度卡片，把“被动记忆”到“后台主动综合”的变化讲得更容易转发。
- 旅途安排和每周资产更新移进 Calendar，这比散落在提醒事项里更适合当前的跨设备事实源。
- 今天的市场信号继续带 target、失效条件和 regime，后面复盘时可以判断观点有没有被现实打脸，而不是只重读一段分析。
- 今天最大的 builder lesson：自动化不只要验证被改的东西，也要验证那些“不该变”的东西；安静的 bug 往往藏在那里。
