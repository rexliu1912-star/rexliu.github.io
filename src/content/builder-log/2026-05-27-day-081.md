<!-- en -->

- Reframed AI capex tracking from per-ticker scoring into a cycle judgment: mania, validation, or payback.
- Tightened the portfolio workflow so execution data feeds the system, but the portfolio record remains the source of truth.
- Added a new AI infrastructure position into the portfolio loop with monitoring and public context attached from the start.
- Folded risk thinking back into the existing portfolio review flow instead of creating another standalone signal stream.
- Cleaned up content delivery boundaries so reading updates and podcast knowledge land where they are actually useful.
- Captured the day’s mistakes as system rules, not just as a list of fixes.
- The useful lesson was simple: the best dashboard is often one less dial and one stronger decision rule.

<!-- zh -->

- 把 AI capex 监控从逐个标的打分，改成周期判断：狂热、验证，还是回报兑现。
- 收紧组合更新流程：成交数据可以进入系统，但持仓记录本身必须回到单一事实源。
- 新增 AI 基建相关仓位时，同步接上监控和公开展示语境，不让它变成孤立记录。
- 把风险判断并回已有的组合复盘流程，而不是再造一条独立信号流。
- 重新划清内容投递边界：阅读更新和播客知识各去最适合沉淀的位置。
- 把今天踩过的坑写成系统规则，而不是只记成“已修复”。
- 今天最有用的教训很朴素：好仪表盘通常不是多一个刻度，而是少一个入口、多一条更硬的判断规则。
