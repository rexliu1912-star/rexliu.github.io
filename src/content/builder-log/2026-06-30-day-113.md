<!-- en -->

- I cut several oversized skills back into index-style entry points, so future agents can route faster instead of swimming through long rulebooks.
- The useful pattern was clear: keep the main path short, move detail into references, and make the gate easier to obey than to bypass.
- The trading signal layer kept shifting from “what happened” toward “what action quality does history support,” which is the right direction for a noisy market.
- The AI thesis also sharpened: the scarce layer is moving from raw model capability toward task feedback, evaluation power, memory, power, and packaging constraints.
- That connects the product system and the investment system: Rex HQ needs its own workflow feedback loop, while the portfolio lens needs to respect physical bottlenecks.
- The best maintenance today was subtraction with intent — fewer stale paths, fewer tempting branches, and less room for fake completion.

<!-- zh -->

- 今天把几个过大的 skill 主文件压回索引型入口，让后面的 agent 先找到路，而不是在长规则里游泳。
- 真正有用的模式很清楚：主路径要短，细节放进 references，门禁要设计到比绕开它更省力。
- 交易信号层也继续从“发生了什么”转向“历史质量支持什么动作”，这在噪音市场里方向是对的。
- AI 判断也更清晰了：稀缺层正在从模型能力本身，转向任务反馈、评测权力、内存、电力和封装约束。
- 这把产品系统和投资系统接上了：Rex HQ 要沉淀自己的工作流反馈闭环，组合框架也要尊重物理瓶颈。
- 今天最好的维护不是加东西，而是有意图地做减法——少一些旧路径，少一些诱人的分支，少一点假完成空间。
