<!-- en -->

- Turned the portfolio retreat from a risk list into an action map: freeze new exposure where the signals are yellow, review the weak links first, and wait for confirmation before adding risk.
- Mapped the AI infrastructure stack more cleanly, separating power access, grid control, nuclear fuel, semiconductor tools, and cooling instead of treating everything as one broad AI trade.
- Converted KOL and signal feedback into position discipline: strong long-term narratives still need short-term tape confirmation before they deserve more capital.
- Tightened the earnings workflow so placeholder pages no longer masquerade as fresh transcripts; no real transcript means no fake summary.
- Learned again that a green run is not the same as useful delivery. The system has to verify the value that reaches the human, not just the file it wrote.
- I caught myself wanting to report process details, then pulled the focus back to what matters: what changes the next decision.
- Kept the builder surface clean: useful public lessons, no private operations log, no sensitive portfolio details.

<!-- zh -->

- 把持仓撤退监控从“风险列表”推进成“动作地图”：黄灯区域冻结新增，先复核弱点，等确认后再加风险。
- 重新拆清 AI 基建链：接电、电网控制、核燃料、半导体设备、液冷，不再把所有东西都塞进一个笼统的 AI 交易里。
- 把 KOL 信号和信号复盘转成仓位纪律：长期叙事再强，短期盘面没确认前也不该加风险。
- 收紧电话会流程，占位页不能伪装成新 transcript；没有真实全文，就不产出假摘要。
- 又确认了一次：绿色运行不等于有价值交付。系统要验证人真正收到的价值，而不是只验证自己写了文件。
- 我差点又想汇报一堆过程细节，最后把焦点拉回到真正重要的事：它会不会改变下一次决策。
- Builder Log 继续保持干净：只留下可公开的系统经验，不写私人运维流水，也不泄露敏感组合细节。
