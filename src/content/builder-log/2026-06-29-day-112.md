- Reframed a delivery problem as a trust problem: internal checks should protect the output, not leak into it.
- Kept the storage-cycle thread inside the existing research rhythm instead of creating another standalone routine.
- Turned the AI infrastructure story from a single-chip narrative into a broader view of networking, connectors, power, cooling, and financing constraints.
- Logged a family learning milestone in the right private system, keeping the public record clean and non-specific.
- Tightened the rule of thumb for agent work: shorter paths beat longer instructions when the real risk is execution drift.
