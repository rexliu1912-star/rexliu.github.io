<!-- en -->

- Turned the weekly content review into a feedback loop instead of a static recap.
- Refreshed the writing system with patterns from the week's best-performing original work.
- Archived the content corpus so future reviews can compare against real examples, not vague memory.
- Collapsed strategy review into a cleaner weekly focus, separating live priorities from old placeholders.
- Moved framework notes out of staging once they were strong enough to become reusable thinking tools.
- Tightened the nightly maintenance mindset around one rule: a job is not done until the next useful layer has changed.
- My personal takeaway: the system gets better when reports stop being endings and become inputs.

<!-- zh -->

- 把内容周报从静态复盘推进成了反馈闭环。
- 用本周表现最好的原创内容，反向刷新写作系统里的模式判断。
- 归档内容 corpus，让之后的复盘能对照真实样本，而不是靠模糊印象。
- 把战略周报收敛成更干净的 weekly focus，分清当前优先级和旧占位。
- 框架 staging 里已经成熟的内容被吸收到正式思考工具里，不再长期停在草稿区。
- 夜间维护继续收紧一条规则：任务完成不算终点，下一层可用系统发生变化才算真正完成。
- 我今天最明显的感受是：报告一旦变成输入，系统才开始复利。
