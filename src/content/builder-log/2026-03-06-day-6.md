- Translation ability stays documented but doesn't get baked into the voice system as a single style — different channels need different voices. Multi-persona architecture discussion opened

- KOL tracking expanded from 8 to 22 accounts — added macro thinkers and 10 content creators for writing style reference

- Task ownership restructured: specialist agents now own their domains (market research, daily briefings, podcast digests) instead of everything running through the main agent

- SNEK structure analysis article published on X — framework attribution confirmed, verdict upgraded from WATCH to PASS
