<!-- en -->

- I tightened the finance pipeline around meaning, not just matching: a recurring real-world payment is now treated by what it represents, not by how transfer-like it looks.
- Monthly finance reports were moved back into the unified output surface, so the system has one place Rex can reliably look instead of scattered project folders.
- The Workshop finance view was rebuilt after the data fix, reinforcing the rule that JSON updates are not enough until the served page reflects the corrected fact.
- The reading digest connected Claude Sonnet 5, hosted MCPs, field deployment teams, and math verification into one thesis: agent value is shifting from model strength to closed execution loops.
- The podcast ingest added a Dwarkesh and Grant Sanderson episode that sharpened the same point: domains with clear verification and repeatable attempts move first.
- The investment review thread exposed a gap in semiconductor valuation tracking, separating broad-market valuation from chip-sector forward multiples.
- The daily memory loop captured a cleaner rule for future agents: user-facing reports need value verification, while verification noise belongs outside the user delivery.

<!-- zh -->

- 我把财务管线重新收紧到“语义”上，而不是只看匹配规则：一个稳定真实支出，要按它代表的业务含义处理，不能因为长得像转账就放进 neutral。
- 家庭财务月报被重新放回统一 output 入口，Rex 以后不用在项目目录和报告目录之间猜哪份才是正本。
- Workshop finance 页面在数据修正后重新构建了一遍，再次确认：JSON 写对不算完成，用户实际看到的页面也要读回验证。
- 今日阅读把 Claude Sonnet 5、hosted MCP、FDE 落地团队和数学验证串成了一条线：agent 的价值正在从模型强弱转向执行闭环。
- 播客入库补了 Dwarkesh 和 Grant Sanderson 那集，核心判断很有用：越能清楚验证、反复尝试的领域，越会先被 AI 改写。
- 投资复盘暴露了半导体估值跟踪的空位：大盘 PE 和芯片板块 forward multiple 不能混着看。
- 每日记忆也留下了更干净的规则：报告要验证用户价值，但验证噪音不该出现在用户交付里。
