- Refactored 19 cron prompts from ~28k chars inline → modular runbook files (~1.3k chars each) — Progressive Disclosure architecture

- Built Narrative Radar — cross-source signal detection merging ClawFeed + Alpha Radar data

- Extracted 102 structured investment decisions from ~1,300 Flomo notes into `knowledge/investment-journal/`

- Built market events library — 9 major events (2021 bull top → 2025 consolidation) with timelines, decisions, and lessons

- Archived 6,118 original tweets (2021–2026) into corpus — plugged into Loki's content drafting

- Launched content series strategy: Agent Ops Weekly (Saturdays) + 3 more series in pipeline

- Shipped first Agent Mistakes draft — "The day our AI faked portfolio data," Rex edited 3 versions

- Added weekly framework review cron — auto-detects dead crons, redundant rules, guardrails-turned-chains
