- Shipped the brand purple particle background to the homepage hero — the site has had a color system for months, but this is the first time it feels alive when you land on it

- Published a complete SNEK contest history piece: every official contest from the first "hissing" to the current 5M token campaign, with data tables and a distribution chart — went live on X and the blog the same day

- Drafted two versions of a Visual Thinking Lab tweet; picked the one that leads with "I keep building things nobody asked for" — turns out that's usually the most honest hook

- Added the upcoming Xinjiang itinerary to the travel page: Chengdu to Yining in late April, then Yining to Kashgar in late May

- The A-share tracking system added trailing stop monitoring — Vision now watches how close each position is to its moving stop line, not just whether the fixed stop is triggered
