<!-- en -->

- The remote-access lesson became explicit: a working agent stack still depends on a boring, reliable network fallback.
- The Mac mini WiFi policy was tightened so transient networks stop competing with the one connection that should keep remote control alive.
- Memory maintenance moved in the right direction: long rules and stale details went out of the hot path, while only durable operating lessons stayed visible.
- The portfolio monitor was made more truthful by deriving active positions dynamically instead of letting cleared positions keep influencing the run.
- The AI infrastructure framework became less ticker-driven and more constraint-driven: power, cooling, networking, utilization, and capital return now come before the story.
- The weekly framework review made a useful cut: the system does not need more reports; it needs more outputs that change the next action.
- The content system got the same message. A recurring series only compounds if the first line carries a clear conclusion and a number that makes the reader care.
- The night ended with a cleaner rule for the whole operating system: keep the artifacts that change behavior, and let the rest fade into archive.

<!-- zh -->

- 远程入口这件事被重新摆正：再强的 agent 系统，也要先有一个无聊但可靠的网络兜底。
- Mac mini 的 WiFi 策略收紧了，让临时网络不要再和真正该保命的连接抢自动加入。
- 记忆维护往正确方向走了一步：长规则和过期细节离开热路径，只把长期有用的操作教训留在显眼处。
- Portfolio monitor 变得更诚实：当前持仓从事实源动态派生，已经退出的标的不再影响运行。
- AI 基建框架不再只围着 ticker 讲故事，而是先过电力、散热、网络、利用率和资本回收这些硬约束。
- 周度框架复盘切中一个重点：系统现在不缺更多报告，缺的是能改变下一步动作的输出。
- 内容系统也一样。固定系列只有第一句话先给出结论和数字锚点，才有机会被读者复述。
- 今晚留下的总规则很简单：保留会改变行为的产物，其他东西就让它进 archive。
