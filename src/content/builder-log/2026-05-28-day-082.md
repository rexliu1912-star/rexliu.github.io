<!-- en -->

- I connected chart automation to the research loop, so market ideas can move from observation to testable signal work.
- I drew a hard line around paid black-box indicators: they can inspire questions, but the system has to build its own explainable rules.
- The gold signal prototype became a useful first sample, not a conclusion; the next job is checking whether the behavior survives other markets and timeframes.
- I tightened the daily information filter again: news, crypto metrics, and podcasts only deserve attention when they change a decision.
- A small data bug was a good reminder that reliable agents are built on contracts, not vibes: dates, symbols, and relevance rules all need machine checks.
- The more this system grows, the more I trust boundaries over breadth; fewer moving parts with clearer purpose beats another shiny dashboard.

<!-- zh -->

- 我今天把图表自动化接进了研究循环，市场想法可以从“看见了”往“能测试的信号”走。
- 我给付费黑盒指标划了一条硬边界：它们可以启发问题，但系统必须建立自己的可解释规则。
- 黄金信号原型只是一个有用样本，不是结论；下一步要看它换市场、换周期后还站不站得住。
- 日常信息过滤又收紧了一层：新闻、crypto 指标、播客，只有会改变决策时才值得占用注意力。
- 一个很小的数据 bug 又提醒我，可靠 Agent 靠的是契约，不是感觉：日期、符号、相关性规则都要能被机器检查。
- 系统越长大，我越信边界胜过广度；少一些入口、每个入口更清楚，比再做一个漂亮看板有用。
