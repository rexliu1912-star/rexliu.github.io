<!-- en -->
- Built journey replay — hit ▶ and the map flies through every city in chronological order, from the very first stop to today. Historical cities included, not just the nomad leg.

- Added a live counter to the current city — shows how many days, hours, minutes, seconds we've been in Chengdu. Ticks every second.

- Previewed the next stop on the map — a dashed line points from Chengdu toward Xi'an, with a badge counting down to departure.

- Replaced the single purple color scheme with a regional palette — Yunnan gets teal, Guangxi gets amber, Fujian gets sky blue, Sichuan gets red. The route lines follow the city colors. Historical cities join the palette too, no more grey.

- Added marker hover effects — hovering scales the marker up, clicking flies to the city with a more cinematic zoom-in.

- Cleaned up the data — removed two spots we haven't actually visited yet. The map should only show places we've been.

<!-- zh -->
- 做了旅途播放功能 —— 点 ▶ 地图会自动按时间顺序飞经所有城市，从第一站一路到现在，历史城市也纳进去了，不只是游牧那段。

- 给当前城市加了实时计时器 —— 显示在成都待了多少天几小时几分几秒，每秒跳动。

- 在地图上预告了下一站 —— 从成都画了一条虚线指向西安，右上角有个徽标倒计时出发日期。

- 把单一紫色方案换成了区域配色 —— 云南翠绿、广西橙金、福建海蓝、四川辣红，路线颜色跟着城市走。历史城市也有自己的颜色，不再是灰的。

- 加了 marker 悬停动效 —— hover 放大，点击飞入城市时有更强的镜头推进感。

- 清理了一些数据 —— 删掉了还没去过的景点。地图只该显示真正踩过的地方。
