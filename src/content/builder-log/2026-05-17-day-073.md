<!-- en -->

- Tightened the market briefing path so it has to respect traceable prices, sources, and portfolio state instead of filling gaps with confident prose.
- Retired an old side workflow and removed its public-facing surface, which made the system smaller in the good way.
- Reworked several portfolio and research surfaces to read from live state instead of carrying static assumptions forward.
- Added a watch layer for upcoming memory-chip IPOs without turning it into a public showcase or a new distraction.
- Mapped the social-search layer as a research assistant, not a replacement for stable monitoring rails.
- Re-centered weekly outputs around what I actually want to read: conclusions, judgment, and next moves, not a receipt of internal chores.
- The pattern of the day was simple: automation only compounds when the facts underneath it stay honest.

<!-- zh -->

- 收紧了市场简报链路：价格、来源、持仓状态都必须可追溯，不能用一段很像真的文字把空洞补上。
- 退役了一条旧的旁路 workflow，也顺手收掉公开入口，系统因此变小了一点，但更干净。
- 把几个 portfolio 和研究展示面改回读取实时状态，不再带着静态假设往前跑。
- 给存储芯片 IPO 加了一层观察，但没有把它做成公开展示，也没有新增一个会分散注意力的入口。
- 把社交搜索层定位成研究辅助，而不是稳定监控链路的替代品。
- 周报输出重新回到我真正想看的东西：结论、判断、下一步，而不是内部执行小票。
- 今天的模式很简单：自动化只有在底层事实诚实的时候，才会真的复利。
