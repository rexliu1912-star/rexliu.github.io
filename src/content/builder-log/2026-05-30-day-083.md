<!-- en -->

- The portfolio system got a new constraint: before suggesting another related buy, it now checks whether the money source and allocation gap actually support that move.
- The investment map found several blind spots around AI infrastructure, optical links, nuclear fuel, and security, but the real output was not a shopping list; it was a cleaner research queue.
- I kept the TradingView direction on the right side of the line: visible labels can train our thinking, but the durable path is an explainable signal system we can test ourselves.
- A long crypto conversation was distilled into a simple discipline question: is the core position a network adoption bet, or just a volatility trade dressed up as conviction?
- Several large skills were cut back into index-style entry points, with the heavy material moved into references so the next agent loads only what it needs.
- The finance workflow processed the monthly files end to end: parse, dedupe, classify, update data, refresh the dashboard, archive the inputs, and verify the public page.
- The website deployment boundary was tightened again: this site belongs to the Cloudflare Pages flow, so the right finish line is a clean git push and a verified page.
- The evening review pulled the day back into one usable thread: market risks, research gaps, knowledge intake, and system maintenance all translated into next actions.

<!-- zh -->

- Portfolio 系统多了一层真实约束：再建议同源加仓前，先看资金来源和配置缺口是不是支撑这个动作。
- 投资地图找到了 AI 基建、光互连、核燃料和安全层的盲区，但真正的产出不是一串 ticker，而是一条更干净的研究队列。
- TradingView 这条线继续守住边界：可见标签可以拿来训练判断，但长期路线必须是可解释、可回测的自研信号系统。
- 一期 crypto 长访谈最后被压成一个纪律问题：核心仓到底是网络采用押注，还是把波动交易包装成信仰？
- 几个大 skill 被砍回索引型入口，重内容下沉到 references，让下一次 agent 只加载真正需要的东西。
- 家庭账单流程走完了完整闭环：解析、去重、分类、更新数据、刷新页面、归档源文件，再验证页面可见。
- 网站部署边界再次收紧：这个站走 Cloudflare Pages，正确终点是干净 push 和页面验证，不是换一套部署工具。
- 晚间复盘把今天重新收束成一条能用的线：市场风险、研究缺口、知识摄入和系统维护，最后都要落到下一步动作。
