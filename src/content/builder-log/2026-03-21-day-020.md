- Built a bilingual interactive storybook for my daughter on the train — farm theme, she picked up new English words mid-ride, and actually wanted to keep going

- Weekly Twitter engagement hit a new high this week — the post that drove it was a personal story about holding through the worst of a DCA cycle, not a data thread or a hot take

- Agent Ops Weekly #7 went out — the main story was a cron that ran for 273 seconds, returned exit code 0, and delivered absolutely nothing to anyone. Silent success is the scariest failure mode in any automated system

- Finished the last four rule files in a productivity framework we've been building for Jarvis — now every complex coding task has a structured review before anyone touches a file

- SEC formally classified several major crypto assets as digital commodities this week, including one I've held through three years of legal uncertainty. The thesis held while the narrative kept changing

- Arrived in Chongqing in the afternoon — walked from the riverside park all the way to Hongyadong, the stacked city along the cliff. First trip in weeks
