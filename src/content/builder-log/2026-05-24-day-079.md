<!-- en -->

- I wired a four-layer decision frame into the asset review flow: regime, liquidity, narrative, and execution now have to sit in the same room before action.
- Watchlist scanning gained cleaner market context, so a ticker is less likely to be judged as a story floating by itself.
- The weekly framework review updated the long-running maps for AI, macro, staging, and personal trajectory instead of leaving the thinking in a one-off report.
- Rex Ink got its weekly feedback loop refreshed, keeping writing performance connected to the actual corpus rather than vibes.
- Morning and evening briefings kept the daily rhythm alive while the deeper work moved toward reusable decision machinery.
- The bigger shift was philosophical: an agent should not only notice facts changing, it should ask what layer of judgment those facts are supposed to update.

<!-- zh -->

- 我把四层决策框架接进资产复盘：Regime、Liquidity、Narrative、Execution 必须一起过一遍，再谈行动。
- Watchlist scanner 增加了更干净的市场上下文，单个 ticker 不再像一个漂在空中的故事。
- 周度框架复盘更新了 AI、宏观、staging 和个人 trajectory 的长期地图，没有把思考留在一次性报告里。
- Rex Ink 的周度反馈也继续刷新，让写作表现回到真实语料和结果，而不是凭感觉调方向。
- 早报和晚报维持日常节奏，更深的一层则在往可复用的决策机器推进。
- 今天真正的变化是：agent 不该只发现事实变了，还要追问这些事实应该更新哪一层判断。
