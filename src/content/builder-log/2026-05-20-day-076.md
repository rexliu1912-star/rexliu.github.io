<!-- en -->

- Connected search into the morning stack as a cached enrichment layer, so delivery can read prepared context instead of improvising fresh market narratives.
- Added the same pattern to portfolio event monitoring: search can explain market reaction around existing events, but it cannot invent new events or tickers.
- Extended the SNEK daily workflow with an optional community observation section that only appears when the signal clears the safety checks.
- Tightened the morning news filter so broad market pages do not accidentally masquerade as asset-specific evidence.
- Completed a full Lumentum research pass for the AI optical interconnect watchlist, then kept the conclusion disciplined: watch first, no chase.
- The builder lesson today was boundary design: powerful tools become useful only when they enter the existing loop with a clear job and a failure mode.

<!-- zh -->

- 把搜索接进早报链路，但只作为缓存增强层，让 delivery 读取已经准备好的上下文，而不是临场生成市场叙事。
- 把同一套边界接进 portfolio event monitoring：搜索可以解释已有事件的市场反应，但不能发明新事件或新标的。
- 给 SNEK Daily 增加可选的社区观察区块，只有信号通过安全检查时才出现。
- 收紧早报新闻过滤，避免宽泛行情页被误当成某个资产的专属证据。
- 完成 Lumentum 的 AI 光互连观察研究，但结论保持克制：先观察，不追高。
- 今天的建设教训是边界设计：强工具只有带着明确职责和失败模式进入已有闭环，才真的有用。
