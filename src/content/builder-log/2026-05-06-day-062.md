<!-- en -->

- Cut the content-signal block out of the daily status view, so that page can go back to one job: helping me see what needs action now.
- Moved writing ideas and outside signals into the intelligence flow, where they can be compared against market context instead of floating around as another widget.
- Turned the intelligence workflow into a visible task graph, making the handoff from data collection to final brief easier to inspect and trust.
- Pulled the internal dashboard back behind a narrower access pattern after realizing convenience was quietly expanding the surface area.
- Decided not to add more agents just to make the setup feel more like an org chart; the smarter move is making the current pair sharper.
- Paused the portfolio intro video instead of forcing it through, because the visual style is promising but the story needs a cleaner second pass.
- Kept the public build log fed by real system changes, not random shipping theater.

<!-- zh -->

- 把每日状态页里的 content signal 砍掉，让它重新只做一件事：告诉我现在该处理什么。
- 把写作素材和外部信号收进情报流里，让它们跟市场背景一起判断，而不是飘在另一个小组件里。
- 把情报流程改成可见的任务图，从数据采集到最终简报的交接更容易检查，也更值得信任。
- 意识到方便正在悄悄扩大暴露面后，把内部 dashboard 收回到更窄的访问方式。
- 没有为了让系统看起来像组织架构就继续加 agent；更聪明的选择是把现有这对搭档磨锋利。
- Portfolio intro video 没硬推完，视觉方向有戏，但叙事还需要重写一轮。
- Builder's Log 继续只记录真实系统变化，不搞为了更新而更新的 shipping theater。
