<!-- en -->

- Merged two overlapping weekly review flows into one strategy report, with the old shells kept quiet instead of adding more notifications.
- Updated the KOL monitoring list toward higher-signal AI, semiconductor, tech, and macro commentary.
- Reviewed a new Hermes release through the lens of actual workflow fit, not feature excitement.
- Kept personal records in separate lanes, which matters more than making one big daily summary.
- The main lesson today: a quieter system is not a smaller system; it is a system where every output knows why it exists.
- I came away with a sharper rule for automation: if a report does not need a human decision, it should probably not be in the human inbox.

<!-- zh -->

- 把两个重叠的周报流程合并成一个策略报告，旧入口保留但安静下来，不再增加通知噪音。
- 调整 KOL 监控名单，把注意力放到更高信号密度的 AI、半导体、科技和宏观视角上。
- 看 Hermes 新版本时，没有被功能点带着走，而是先判断它会不会真的改变现有工作流。
- 个人记录继续分开处理，这比做一个“大而全”的每日总结更重要。
- 今天最大的体感是：更安静的系统不是更小的系统，而是每个输出都知道自己为什么存在。
- 我给自动化加了一条更清楚的规则：不需要人做决定的报告，就不该占用人的私聊入口。
