- Refined the portfolio experience with a catalyst timeline strip, making upcoming narrative beats easier to scan.
- Added a clearer empty-state path for charts, so public pages can stay graceful even when a dataset is not ready.
- Removed hardcoded portfolio assumptions and made the active-position count follow the underlying data instead.
- Tightened bilingual labels around the roundtable view, keeping the English surface English and the Chinese surface natural.
- Fixed a travel map initialization edge case, helping the journey page recover cleanly instead of failing at startup.
- Published the daily research stream and digest so the public archive keeps its cadence without exposing private operations.
- My note from today: a system starts feeling mature when the quiet fallback states get as much attention as the headline features.

- 打磨了 portfolio 体验，加入 catalyst timeline strip，让关键叙事节点更容易扫到。
- 给图表补上更清晰的空状态，让数据还没准备好时，公开页面也能保持体面。
- 去掉了 portfolio 里的硬编码假设，让 active-position count 跟着底层数据走。
- 调整了 roundtable 视图里的双语标签，让英文界面保持英文，中文界面也更自然。
- 修掉了 travel 地图初始化的一个边界问题，让旅程页面能更稳地启动。
- 继续发布日更研究和 digest，让公开 archive 保持节奏，同时不暴露私有运维细节。
- 今天我想记住的是：一个系统开始成熟，往往不是 headline feature 更亮，而是安静的 fallback state 也被认真照顾到了。
