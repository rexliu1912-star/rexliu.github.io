## The Setup

ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 video generation model has triggered a structural shift in short-form drama production. Jeff, founder of AI drama platform JuRiLu, predicts the industry will hit a "one person, one day, one drama" threshold by March–April 2026 — at commercial quality, not just demos.

## Key Takeaways

- **Cost inversion**: Labor used to be 80% of production cost. By mid-2026, compute/tokens will be 80%, labor just 20%.
- **Price floor collapse**: A commercial-grade short drama that cost ¥500,000+ with human actors now costs under ¥200,000 with AI; by Q2, that drops to ¥5,000–50,000.
- **Supply glut incoming**: Oversupply arrives in 2–3 months — but it expands the market, not kills it. Niche demand gets unlocked.
- **Platform logic flips**: Today platforms court content makers. By end of 2026, content makers will need to buy their way onto platforms.
- **Interactive drama**: Real-time AI-generated branching narratives (not pre-scripted) become viable by late 2026 — the key unlock being cost + real-time inference.

## Why It Matters

This is the same pattern playing out in coding, writing, and design — except video was supposed to be "too hard." The cost structure argument is the most important: when compute is the main cost, the marginal cost of the 1,000th episode approaches zero. That changes what content can exist. Niche sports, obscure hobbies, hyper-local stories — anything that needed 50,000 viewers to justify production now needs maybe 500.

For builders: the playbook isn't "make the next viral drama." It's find a tight audience, build a production pipeline, repeat at high frequency. Volume + precision over chasing hits.

**What to watch:**
- Seedance 2.0 and Kling quality comparisons post-March 2026
- Whether any vertical content platform launches on this thesis
- Actor IP licensing becoming normalized (actors selling likeness rights)
- Interactive drama platform launches H2 2026

## 背景

字节跳动旗下视频生成模型 Seedance 2.0 正在推动 AI 短剧走向规模化落地。巨日禄创始人杰夫预判，今年 3—4 月，将出现「一人一天一部剧」的生产能力——达到商业化标准，而非只是 demo。

## 关键要点

- **成本结构倒挂**：此前人力成本占短剧制作的 80%；2026 年中，算力/Token 将占 80%，人力只占 20%。
- **价格地板崩塌**：真人拍摄成本超 50 万的短剧，AI 版已降至 20 万以内；Q2 起将进入 5000—5 万元的「小万时代」。
- **供给过剩来临**：两三个月后供给过剩，但这扩大了市场而非终结赛道——小众细分需求被激活。
- **平台与内容关系逆转**：如今是平台找内容；2026 年底将变成内容方买量求上线。
- **互动影游机会**：2026 年底 AI 实时后置生成成熟，互动剧商业化成为可能，突破过去「分支只能前置生成、成本高」的双重瓶颈。

## 为什么重要

这和编程、写作、设计的颠覆逻辑一模一样——只是视频被认为「更难」。最关键的论点是成本结构：当算力成为主要成本，第 1000 集的边际成本趋近于零。这改变了什么内容能够存在。小众运动、冷门爱好、超本地化故事——以前需要 5 万观众才能回本，现在可能只需要 500 人。

对 Builder 来说：不是去做下一个爆款，而是找到精准受众、搭生产流水线、高频复制。走量 + 精准，而不是赌爆款。
