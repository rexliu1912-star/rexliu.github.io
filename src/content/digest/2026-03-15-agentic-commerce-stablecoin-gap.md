## The Setup

When Citrini Research published a piece arguing stablecoins would disintermediate Visa and Mastercard, card stocks dropped sharply and Crypto Twitter cheered. The thesis sounded clean: AI agents optimize every transaction, interchange is a tax, stablecoins route around it.

Noah Levine at a16z Crypto spent his days in crypto — and wanted it to be right. But most of it is wrong. Not because stablecoins don't matter. Because the real opportunity has nothing to do with replacing cards.

## Why Cards Win Most of This

Cards don't just move money. They extend unsecured credit, pre-authorize uncertain transactions, and guarantee fraud protection with chargeback rights. Stablecoins can move money. They cannot yet do the rest.

- 82% of Americans carry a rewards card. 18 billion cards are in circulation globally.
- Visa has issued over 16 billion tokens — an agent is just another device that carries one.
- Visa's Intelligent Commerce framework is in pilot. Mastercard's Agent Pay is live for all US cardholders.
- Stripe + OpenAI's Agentic Commerce Protocol has Etsy live and 1M+ Shopify merchants coming online.

For existing merchants and consumers, cards will likely dominate agentic commerce. Full stop.

## The Real Stablecoin Opportunity: Merchants That Don't Exist Yet

Every platform shift creates merchants the existing payment system cannot serve — until it adapts. PayPal took 16 years before processors created formal underwriting guidelines for the model it pioneered.

The AI wave is creating these merchants faster than any prior shift:
- 36 million new developers joined GitHub last year alone
- In YC W2025, 25% of companies had codebases 95%+ AI-generated
- On Bolt.new, 67% of 5 million users are **not** developers

A vibe coder builds a financial data tool in four hours. No website. No legal entity. Another agent calls it 40,000 times at $0.001/call — $40 revenue, zero human checkout. The first question is always: **"How do I get paid?"** Right now, most of them can't.

Processors reject them — not because the tech is lacking, but because underwriting requires a merchant profile. No website, no entity, no track record = unacceptable risk.

Protocols like **x402** embed stablecoin payments directly into HTTP requests. No merchant account, no processor, no onboarding, no chargeback liability. These merchants aren't choosing stablecoins over cards. They're choosing stablecoins over **nothing**.

## Why It Matters

The pattern is consistent across every platform shift: new merchant waves come first, underwriting catches up later. In that gap, stablecoins are the infrastructure.

Cards serve every merchant a processor **can** underwrite. Stablecoins serve every merchant a processor **cannot**. The next wave of commerce will be built in that gap — and vibe coders, AI-native tools, and autonomous agent services are already living there.

**What to watch:**
- x402 protocol adoption across AI developer tooling
- How quickly Visa/Mastercard extend agent token support
- Whether stablecoin payment rails (Circle, Base) capture the "new merchant" cohort before processors adapt

## 背景

前段时间，Citrini Research 发了篇报告说稳定币会取代 Visa 和 Mastercard，卡公司股票当天应声大跌，Crypto Twitter 一片欢呼。逻辑听起来很干净：AI agent 会优化每一笔交易，跨行手续费就是税，稳定币绕过去就行了。

a16z Crypto 的 Noah Levine 自己在 crypto 圈混，也希望这个逻辑是对的。但大部分都是错的。不是因为稳定币不重要，而是真正的机会根本不在于取代信用卡。

## 为什么信用卡会赢大部分

信用卡不只是搬钱。它提供无担保信贷、预授权不确定金额、带消费纠纷保护。稳定币能搬钱，但其他的暂时做不到。

- 82% 的美国人有奖励信用卡，全球流通 180 亿张
- Visa 已发行超过 160 亿个 token——agent 只是一个新设备，用一样的 token
- Visa Intelligent Commerce 框架在试点，Mastercard Agent Pay 已向所有美国持卡人开放
- Stripe + OpenAI 的 Agentic Commerce Protocol 已有 Etsy 接入，超百万 Shopify 商家即将上线

对现有商户和消费者来说，AI agent 支付的主战场，信用卡大概率是赢家。就这样。

## 稳定币的真正机会：那些还不存在的商户

每次平台浪潮都会制造传统支付体系无法服务的新商户——直到系统慢慢适应。PayPal 开创了聚合商户模式，结果等了16年，行业才有正式的承保标准。

这次 AI 浪潮制造新商户的速度，比任何一次都快：
- 去年一年 GitHub 新增 3600 万开发者
- YC 2025 冬季班里，25% 的公司代码库 95% 以上由 AI 生成
- Bolt.new 的 500 万用户里，67% 根本不是开发者

一个 vibe coder 用四小时做了个金融数据工具，没有网站，没有公司实体。另一个 agent 一周调用了 4 万次，每次 0.001 美元，赚了 40 美元，全程没有人类点击付款页面。这些人的第一个问题永远是：**「怎么收钱？」** 现在，大多数人收不了。

原因不是技术，是风控。支付处理商不接受没有网站、没有实体、没有记录的商户。

**x402 协议**直接把稳定币支付嵌进 HTTP 请求。不需要商户账号、不需要处理商、不需要审核、没有退款风险。这些人不是在「选稳定币 vs 信用卡」，他们是在「选稳定币 vs 什么都没有」。

## 为什么重要

历史规律很清楚：新商户浪潮先来，支付体系的承保能力后到。在这个空档期，稳定币就是基础设施。

信用卡服务每一个支付体系**能承保**的商户；稳定币服务每一个支付体系**无法承保**的商户。下一波商业浪潮就在那道缝里建起来——vibe coder、AI 原生工具、自主 agent 服务，都已经住在里面了。

**值得关注：**
- x402 协议在 AI 开发者工具中的渗透速度
- Visa/Mastercard 的 agent token 支持范围扩展进度
- Circle、Base 等稳定币支付轨道，能否在传统处理商适应前锁定「新商户」群体
