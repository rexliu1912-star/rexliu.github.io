## The Setup

Two milestones landed in Cardano's March 31 community digest. First: **Midnight Network** — the privacy sidechain built by IOG — officially launched mainnet. Second: **FluidTokens** executed the first-ever native Bitcoin–Cardano atomic swap without a bridge or custodian.

These aren't small updates. For anyone holding ADA or SNEK, this is the kind of ecosystem activity that matters.

## Key Takeaways

- **Midnight goes live.** The sidechain uses zero-knowledge proofs to enable selective disclosure — you can prove ownership or compliance without revealing underlying data. It's built to attract regulated DeFi and enterprise use cases that current Cardano can't serve.
- **BTC–ADA atomic swap.** FluidTokens used Hash Time Locked Contracts (HTLCs) to swap Bitcoin and Cardano natively — no wrapped tokens, no bridge risk, no custodian. This is technically significant: it means BTC liquidity can flow into Cardano DeFi directly.
- **Cardano Node 10.7.0 pre-release** is also out, with an SPO call scheduled for April 2. Steady infrastructure progress.

## Why It Matters

Cardano has been criticized for years as a chain with potential but no activity. The DeFi/liquidity question is real — the ecosystem needs TVL and real users to matter.

Midnight changes the addressable market. Privacy is a genuine unsolved problem in crypto — whether for institutions (GDPR compliance), DAOs (governance privacy), or individuals (financial privacy). If Midnight executes, Cardano starts competing in a space where Ethereum's transparency is actually a *weakness*.

The BTC atomic swap is more immediately impactful. Bitcoin has the deepest liquidity in crypto. Getting even a fraction of that into Cardano DeFi natively — without the trust assumptions of a bridge — would change the TVL picture entirely.

**What to watch:**
- Midnight developer adoption: how fast do projects start building on it?
- FluidTokens volume: does the BTC–ADA swap see organic usage?
- Cardano Node 10.7.0 full release timeline and what it enables for staking/DeFi

## 背景

Cardano 3 月 31 日社区 digest 同时落地两个里程碑。第一：IOG 旗下的隐私侧链 **Midnight Network** 正式上线主网。第二：**FluidTokens** 完成了史上首个原生 BTC–ADA 原子互换，无桥接，无托管方。

这不是日常小更新。对持有 ADA 或 SNEK 的人来说，这正是生态实质进展的信号。

## 关键要点

- **Midnight 上线。** 该侧链使用零知识证明实现"选择性披露"——你可以证明所有权或合规性，却不暴露底层数据。目标是吸引当前 Cardano 无法服务的受监管 DeFi 和企业场景。
- **BTC–ADA 原子互换。** FluidTokens 通过哈希时间锁合约（HTLCs）实现原生比特币与 Cardano 互换——无包装代币，无桥接风险，无托管方。技术意义重大：BTC 流动性可以直接进入 Cardano DeFi。
- **Cardano Node 10.7.0 预发布版**同期发布，4 月 2 日 SPO 电话会议跟进，基础设施稳步推进。

## 为什么重要

Cardano 多年来饱受"有潜力但没活动"的批评。DeFi/流动性问题是真实存在的——生态需要 TVL 和真实用户才能站得住脚。

Midnight 改变了可触达的市场。隐私是 crypto 尚未解决的真正问题——无论是对机构（GDPR 合规）、DAO（治理隐私），还是个人（财务隐私）。如果 Midnight 能执行落地，Cardano 就开始在一个以太坊透明性反而是弱点的赛道上竞争。

BTC 原子互换更有近期影响力。比特币在 crypto 中拥有最深的流动性。即使只有一小部分原生进入 Cardano DeFi——不带桥接的信任假设——也会彻底改变 TVL 的面貌。
