## The Setup

AI agents can already manage wallets, execute DeFi trades, and move value on-chain. But when it came to the simplest thing — buying something — they hit a wall. That wall just came down.

MoonPay launched the **MoonAgents Card** on Friday: a virtual Mastercard debit card that lets autonomous AI agents spend stablecoins at any online merchant that accepts Mastercard. The card converts crypto to fiat at the point of purchase. No pre-loading into a custodial account. No human in the loop.

Built with Monavate (regulated payments infra) and Exodus (self-custody wallets), the product is live now in the UK and Latin America, with US and EU expansion coming within months.

## Key Takeaways

- **Direct from wallet**: Agents spend from on-chain wallets at transaction time. Wallet custody never leaves the user. Access is revocable at any time.
- **Real adoption signal**: MoonPay CLI has processed over 4 million tool calls. The first million took 30 days. The second million took 7 days.
- **Infrastructure is catching up to ambition**: Fintech is now building plumbing for a world where AI systems — not humans — initiate and complete transactions at machine speed.

## Why It Matters

This isn't just a payments product. It's the closing of a critical loop in the **agentic economy**. Until now, an AI agent could research a product, compare prices, and even execute a DeFi swap to fund the purchase — but couldn't complete the final step. MoonAgents Card bridges on-chain capital to off-chain commerce.

The timing matters. Stablecoin usage is exploding (JPMorgan reported Friday that transaction volume is rising fast even as market cap growth lags). Payment rails for agents are the next logical layer. Whoever controls agent spending infrastructure controls a significant chunk of the future payments stack.

**What to watch:**
- US/EU regulatory path — KYC requirement for card issuance is a friction point
- Whether Mastercard expands this to other crypto partners or keeps exclusivity
- Competitor response: Stripe's Link wallet for agents landed yesterday. The agent payments race is on.

## 背景

AI Agent 已经能管理钱包、执行 DeFi 交易、在链上转移价值。但当它们想做最简单的事——买东西——却碰了壁。这堵墙刚刚倒了。

MoonPay 周五推出了 **MoonAgents Card**：一张虚拟 Mastercard 借记卡，让自主运行的 AI Agent 在任何接受 Mastercard 的在线商户花稳定币。卡片在购买时实时将加密货币兑换为法币。不需要预存到托管账户。不需要人类插手。

产品由 Monavate（受监管的支付基础设施）和 Exodus（自托管钱包）联合打造，目前已在英国和拉美上线，美国和欧盟将在未来几个月内推出。

## 关键要点

- **直接从钱包消费**：Agent 在交易时从链上钱包直接支付。钱包托管权始终在用户手中，随时可撤销 Agent 权限。
- **真实的采用信号**：MoonPay CLI 已处理超过 400 万次工具调用。第一个百万花了 30 天，第二个百万只用了 7 天。
- **基础设施正在追赶野心**：金融科技正在为"AI 系统而非人类发起和完成交易"的未来搭建管道。

## 为什么重要

这不只是一个支付产品。它补上了 **Agent 经济** 关键的一环。在此之前，AI Agent 可以研究商品、比较价格、甚至执行 DeFi 兑换来筹集资金——但无法完成最后一步。MoonAgents Card 把链上资本和链下商业连接了起来。

时机很关键。稳定币使用量正在爆发（JPMorgan 周五报告称交易量激增，尽管市值增长滞后）。面向 Agent 的支付通道是下一层逻辑必然。谁控制了 Agent 的消费基础设施，谁就控制了未来支付栈的相当一部分。

**值得关注：**
- 美国/欧盟的监管路径——发卡需要 KYC 是一个摩擦点
- Mastercard 是否会向其他加密伙伴开放，还是保持独占
- 竞争对手的反应：Stripe 的 Link 钱包昨天刚落地，Agent 支付竞赛已经打响
