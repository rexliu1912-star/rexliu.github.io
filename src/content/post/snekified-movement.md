I watched Pudgy Penguins PFPs take over my timeline today.

Made me think about @snek's recent campaign. We've created 64 custom Snekified PFPs. Built anticipation. Created something special.

But here's the thing that hit me: a third of those acoounts never actually switched.

That stung at first. Then I realized we could have done much better.

The real move is to focus on SNEK believers. The ones who already rep Snek daily. The holders who find creative ways to weave it into their lives. The community members who treat it like an actual identity, not a temporary trend.

Capital games aren't that complicated. It's basically buying some ads on people's profiles like Pudgy Penguins. Sure, those viral movements create noise and expand reach.

But sustainable value? That comes from depth, not breadth.

When someone genuinely wants to represent your project, that means something. When they don't, your custom artwork is wasted effort.

So here's my take: we need to shift direction immediately. Make exclusive PFPs for people who've earned them. Reward consistent voices. Build for quality over quantity.

The more people who genuinely value Snek as part of their identity, the more valuable Snek becomes.
